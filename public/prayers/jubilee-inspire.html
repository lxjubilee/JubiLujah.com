Twenty prayer hours. One Temple walk per album.

The feminine cantor of the Inspire Family carries twenty Messianic prayer albums — each one a 60-minute Temple journey from the gates to the Mercy Seat and back out into the world carrying an answered prayer. Family wounds, the body's healing, the racing heart, the empty plate, the buried friend, the wrong road, the daughter's future, the nation's hurt — every concern that can be brought to Yahuah is here, sung back to Him in Hebraic cantorial form. The locked spine

Every album walks the same Temple journey

Twelve stations, in order: 1. Outer Court Entry — Joy & Thanksgiving · 2. Bronze Altar — Repentance · 3. Bronze Laver — Washing · 4. Holy Place — Intimacy with Ruach HaKodesh · 5. The Menorah — Seven Spirits, Illumination · 6. The Shewbread — Bread of Life · 7. The Incense Altar — Prayer as Aroma · 8. Invitation into Holy of Holies · 9. Before the Mercy Seat — Worship & Surrender · 10. Intercession for Others · 11. Petition for Self · 12. Thanksgiving — Yahuah Hears, Yahuah Answers. Songs 1 and 12 carry the Shema in full; songs 2-11 carry it as a hummed niggun fragment. Songs 10 and 11 contain a literal six-second silence directing the listener to speak names and specifics into the prayer.
