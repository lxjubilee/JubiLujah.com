## Lyrics

[Intro — banjo roll + boot stomp, ≤0:08]
(Hey!) Shout it down the holler — louder!

[Verse 1]
Woke up grinnin' like a possum eatin' jam,
Heart so full of Jesus that I scared the ham —
Rooster hit the fence rail, then he fell plumb off,
'Cause I bellowed "He's alive!" and the whole barn coughed

[Pre-Chorus]
If a fence post starts to clappin' and a creek can hum,
Then a quiet man's the only kind of fool I won't become

[Chorus]
Shout it down the holler — (louder!)
Wake the sleepin' coves like a thunder-chowder
Tip the mountain over, let the echo run —
Make God famous to the settin' of the sun!

[Verse 2]
Granny dropped her snuff and she grabbed a tambourine,
Hadn't moved that fast since nineteen-seventeen —
Hound dog started howlin' in a holy key,
Now the whole dern hillside's harmonizin' off of me

[Pre-Chorus]
Don't need a steeple or a Sunday-polished shoe,
Just a wide-open throat and a God who carried you

[Chorus]
Stomp it down the holler — (wilder!)
Kick the dust to glory like a tent-revival fire-builder
Throw your hat at heaven, watch it never come down —
His name's the loudest blessing rollin' through this town

[Bridge — strip to clawhammer banjo + lone voice, then build]
I'll whisper it once... then I can't whisper no more —
The hush breaks open and it rattles every door,
I'll out-holler the thunder till His name's all I am,
Till the dead pines wake and the river hollers "amen!"

[Final Chorus — full stomp + handclaps + fiddle break]
Holler it on, brother — (famous!)
Till the mute hills sing and the meek hills shame us
Shake the rafters loose, let the gladness drown —
Make God famous from the holler to the crown!
(Hey!) Shout it down the holler — His name won't quiet down!
