## Lyrics

[Intro — banjo + stomp, ≤0:08]
Heaven's gettin' full — scoot over!

[Verse 1]
News hit the party-line, eleven necks leaned in:
Tucker's wildest boy just turned his back on sin —
Preacher tried to count 'em, threw his pencil down,
By dinner half the jailhouse had a brand-new crown

[Pre-Chorus]
Angels packin' chairs in like a county fair,
Hollerin' "we need acreage — there's revival in the air!"

[Chorus]
Heaven's gettin' full — (scoot over!)
Jesus keeps on haulin' 'em from ditch and clover
Lamps a-poppin' on in every windowpane,
And the Lamb just laughed and added one more name!

[Verse 2]
Mean old Copperhead Gus poured his whiskey in the weeds,
Said "the Lamb done bought me forty acres I don't need" —
Now the man who cussed at sunrise is doin' a jig in the pew,
If glory made room for Gus, it's holdin' one for you

[Pre-Chorus]
Stretch the table longer, knock a wall plumb out,
Nobody at that door ever gets turned about

[Chorus]
Heaven's gettin' full — (pull up a chair!)
Standing-room-and-stompin' way up the golden stair
Saints a-hangin' off the rafters, grinnin' ear to ear,
'Cause the Shepherd's still out fetchin' and the harvest's here

[Bridge — build]
There's a chair with my name carved before the world began,
There's a chair with your name — quit standin' there, man!
Hear the screen door of heaven bang against the wall —
I'm sprintin' for a seat the angels carved before I asked at all!

[Final Chorus — key change, full stomp]
Heaven's gettin' full — (save me a spot!)
He's loadin' up the wagon with the whole sorry lot
Crank the lamps to blazin', run the dark off the hill —
The Lord ain't done a-savin', and He never will!
Heaven's gettin' full — so quit dawdlin', y'all!

## Scoring Justification

No line repeats — the response mutates ("scoot over" → "pull up a chair" → "save me a spot") and each chorus brings fresh images (ditch-and-clover, golden stair). Jesus is the active rescuer ("the Lamb just laughed and added one more name"), holding Fully God-Centered.
