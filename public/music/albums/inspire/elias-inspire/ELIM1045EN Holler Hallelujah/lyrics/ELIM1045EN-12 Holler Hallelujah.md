## Lyrics

[Intro — stomp + clap, ≤0:05]
Holler — (Hallelujah!)

[Verse 1]
Floor's bowin' like a trampoline under a hundred boots,
Mason jars are marchin' off the sill from the bass-line's roots —
We hiked up this ridge with His name caught in the wind,
And we ain't sittin' down till the whole wide world's grinnin'

[Pre-Chorus]
House so full the walls are breathin' in and out,
Just one word left big enough to shout

[Chorus]
Holler — (Hallelujah!)
Stomp the floorboards loose — (Hallelujah!)
From the cricket-buzzin' cove to the crown of the hill,
A lantern swingin' wild and a joy you can't kill!

[Verse 2]
Empty chairs got faces, every runaway got found,
Heaven's house is packed and it's the fattest, gladdest sound —
So we sling it back to Jesus, every stomp and shout,
Made His name so famous now we'll never wear it out

[Pre-Chorus]
Catch your breath, you mountains, then we go again,
Loud as the mornin' light this whole thing begin

[Bridge — stop-time clap break, crowd only, then flood]
(claps) I didn't earn this hallelujah — He won it and handed it over,
(claps) too good to keep, too big to whisper, spillin' off the clover —
(claps) loud for the lost ones, loud for the late ones stumblin' in,
(full band floods) every daughter, every son, let the whole arbor ring!

[Final Chorus — max stomp, full crowd]
Holler it once more — (Hallelujah!)
Till the rafters quit and the new day's true — (Hallelujah!)
From the dark cove bottom to the bright hill's brim,
We made God famous — now go and brag on Him!
(Hey!) Shout it down the holler — His name won't quiet down!
