Theological guardrails (sensitive themes)

Four sensitive-theme albums verified clean

Tradition (Catholic, Orthodox, Hispanic-Pentecostal) sometimes differs from Bible-only evangelical practice around the saints, Mary, sacred images, and intercession. Santiago's protocol position: affirm the genuine love and reverence behind the practice, direct all worship/prayer/ultimate trust to God alone through Jesus Christ, honor saints and Mary as faithful examples to imitate, do not position any creature as object of worship or mediator replacing Christ (1 Tim 2:5).

SAIM1025 Cuánto Te Adoro · Bolero · 94.5

All adoration EXCLUSIVELY to Jesús. The bolero tradition has rich romantic-adoration language; here the Beloved (Amado) is ALWAYS Jesús.

  • "Amado" strictly = Jesús (Song of Songs 5:16 corporate-Bride, NOT erotic, NOT individualistic)
