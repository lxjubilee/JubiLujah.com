SONG-LEVEL EMOTIONAL ARC

- Establish: The listener arrives at the Table of Showbread and remembers — at the Last Supper, Yeshua gave thanks (eucharisteo) BEFORE He broke the bread; the breaking did not interrupt the thanksgiving
- Escalate: The reframe — "He gave thanks for the bread before He became the bread" — and the listener is invited to thank Yahuah for the breaking before the breaking arrives
- Elevate: Choir blooms, claps lift; the Faith Declaration Bridge declares the Bread received, the body fed, the thanksgiving sealed

---

ANCHOR SCRIPTURES (TWO-WITNESS CONFIRMATION)

- Tanakh: Leviticus 24:5-9 — "you shall take fine flour, and bake twelve cakes thereof... and you shall set them in two rows, six on a row, upon the pure table before Yahuah"
- Brit Hadashah: 1 Corinthians 11:24 — "and when He had given thanks (eucharisteo), He broke it, and said, Take, eat: this is My body, which is broken for you"
- Personalized translation used in lyric: "Yeshua gave thanks for the bread before You broke Him — so I will give thanks for the bread You give me before You break it; I will eucharist the breaking in advance"

SENSORY ANCHOR INVENTORY

1. The smell of twelve fresh loaves laid in two rows of six
2. The sound of bread torn at the seam, then thanked-for again
3. The texture of unleavened cake on the priest's palm warm from the oven
4. The taste of communion bread that has had Yeshua's "thank You" spoken over it before His teeth ever touched it

---

HEBREW WORD AT PEAK

- Lechem HaPanim (לֶחֶם הַפָּנִים) — bread of the Presence / bread of the Faces. Landed at chorus. Plus the Greek "eucharisteo" (gave thanks) honored in the lyric because the Lord's Supper is literally named "Thanksgiving."

HEBREW DICTIONARY

- Amen — "so be it" — a verb of faith-sealing
- Avinu — "our Father"
- Brit Hadashah — "the New Covenant" — the New Testament
- Echad — "One" — the unity confessed in the Shema
- Hineni — "here I am" — the surrender word of Avraham, Moshe, and Yeshayahu when Yahuah called
- Lechem HaPanim — "Bread of the Face / Bread of the Presence" (the Showbread)
- Niggun — wordless melodic prayer-song (lai-lai-lai)
- Ruach — "spirit, breath, wind"
- Shekhinah — the dwelling, manifest Presence of Yahuah
- Shema — "Hear" — opening word of the great Hebrew confession (Deuteronomy 6:4)
- Shewbread — the Bread of the Presence (Lechem HaPanim) on the golden table
- Tanakh — the Hebrew Scriptures — Torah, Prophets, and Writings
- the Lamb — Yeshua as the sacrificial Lamb of Yahuah (Revelation; cf. John 1:29)
- Todah — "thank you / thanksgiving"
- Yahuah — the sacred four-letter Name of the Father (often rendered "the LORD" in English translations)
- Yahuah Echad — "Yahuah is One" (the Shema affirmation)
- Yeshua — the Hebrew name of Jesus, meaning "salvation" or "Yahuah saves"
- Yochanan — John
