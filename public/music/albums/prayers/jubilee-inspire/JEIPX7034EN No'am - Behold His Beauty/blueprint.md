# Album Blueprint - No'am - Behold His Beauty

ALBUM HEADER

- Title: No'am - Behold His Beauty
- Code: JEIPX7034EN
- Persona: Jubilee Inspire
- Theme: To gaze upon the beauty of Yahuah. The aesthetic-theological album - beholding, contemplating, the worship that arises when beauty itself becomes the prayer. 2 Corinthians 3:18 transformation by beholding.
- Content Mode: OHI (Yahuah / Yeshua / Ruach HaKodesh feminine pronouns)
- Album Type: Prayer Album (12-Station Temple Journey, Tefillah B'Shir)
- Anchor Pairing: Psalm 27:4 (Tanakh) + 2 Corinthians 3:18 (Brit Hadashah)
- Runtime Target: 60:00 (12 x 5:00)
- Framework Version: v2.1 (Rules A, B, C, D enforced from line one)

---

THREE-ACT MAPPING

Act 1 - The Threshold of Beholding (Stations 1-3)
The eyes lift. The veil over the heart loosens. Repentance is the first cleansing of vision; consecration is the polishing of the inner mirror.

Act 2 - The Inner Sanctuary of Beauty (Stations 4-9)
The gaze is established. Beauty floods - lamplight, Bread, incense, torn veil, kavod. The face-to-face moment of 2 Corinthians 3:18.

Act 3 - The Beheld-Ones Become Bearers of the Beauty (Stations 10-12)
Beholding produces transformation - the gazer carries the radiance outward. Intercession for others to see Him; petition for personal unveiling; thanksgiving that the beauty has been received.

SHEMA SPINE NOTES

- Track 01: Shema sung in FULL during the niggun bridge (Station 1 mandate)
- Tracks 02-11: Shema carried as melodic fragment within lai-lai-lai humming (recognizable contour, not fully verbalized)
- Track 12: Shema sung in FULL during the niggun bridge (Station 12 mandate, closing confession)
- Melodic identity: D-Phrygian-dominant contour carries the Shema motif. Recurs in every track at the niggun bridge. By minute 60 the listener has heard Israel's oldest sung prayer twelve times in one hour.

---

YESHUA-QUOTED LINE PLACEMENT

Track 11 (Petition for Self) carries a personalized line from the High-Priestly Prayer of John 17:24: "Father, I will that those You have given Me may be with Me where I am, that they may behold My glory." Personalized in the lyric as direct address to the Father: "Yeshua prayed it for me - that I would be with Him where He is, that I would behold His glory - and now I bring His prayer back to You, Avinu." A secondary thread of the Lord's Prayer ("Your Name is holy, hallowed, beautiful") flickers through Track 01.

---

TESTIMONY ANCHOR TRACK

Track 10 (Let Their Eyes Be Opened) carries the testimony anchor. The one who has beheld testifies in the intercession - the beauty seen becomes evidence prayed back over every name lifted: "I have seen Him - and what I have seen is true - so I pray now for the eyes of every name I love."

CINEMATIC REVELATION MOMENT

Faith Declaration Bridge lands the revelation as finished reality.

ALBUM CHARACTER NOTE

The aesthetic-theological prayer album. Where Rapha is the Healer's Hand and other albums petition for change, No'am asks for nothing but the gaze itself - and discovers that the gaze IS the change. Slower than the average prayer album by design; the dwell IS the doctrine. The album proves 2 Corinthians 3:18 in the act of being prayed - by minute 60 the listener has become a little more like the Face she has been beholding.
