[Intro]

with knees that have been locked
since the worry first taught my body how to brace.
And Your Kavod begins to descend —
not light alone, but weight,
the kind of weight that makes a body finally let go
of the lighter weight it has been carrying.
I go to the floor like a coin going home to its slot.
