[Intro]
(whispered, sparse cello drone, single ney flute, no percussion)
Your table is set, Yahuah...

[Verse 1]
I come into Your Holy Place starving, Avinu —
not for food the world bakes
but for the calm
no bakery on earth has ever proofed.
And there is Your table.
Twelve loaves under the linen,
warm enough that the linen has a wrinkle
where the steam is still rising.
And in the middle of Your table
is the Lechem that does not get stale.

By the blood of the Lamb that broke it open,
by the body of Yeshua snapped in two
the same way You snap a loaf
when You sit down with the hungry —
I am invited to Your table
the racing heart has been forgetting to come to.
