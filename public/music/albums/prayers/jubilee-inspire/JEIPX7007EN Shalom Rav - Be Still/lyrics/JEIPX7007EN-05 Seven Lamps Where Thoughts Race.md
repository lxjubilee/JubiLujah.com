[Intro]
(whispered, sparse cello drone, single ney flute, no percussion)
Your lamps are lit, Yahuah...
all seven of Your flames are awake...

[Verse 1]
I come into Your Holy Place
with a mind that has been running
the same six thoughts on a treadmill in the dark.
And You light Your menorah.
Seven flames, leaning a little, like they are listening,
and the wicks of pure beaten oil
begin to read the corridor of my mind
the way a librarian reads a shelf at midnight.

they are looking for the lies the worry built rooms for...
By the blood of the Lamb that lit them,
by the body of Yeshua broken open at noon —
Your Light is in the room
the dark has been hiding in.
