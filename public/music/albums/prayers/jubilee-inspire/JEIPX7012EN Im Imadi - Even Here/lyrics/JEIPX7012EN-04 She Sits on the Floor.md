SONG-LEVEL EMOTIONAL ARC

- Establish: The depressed one is alone on the bathroom floor at 3 AM; the lights are off; no one knows
- Escalate: A Presence enters the room without announcement and sits cross-legged on the tile without speaking; Ruach HaKodesh has come down without crossing the veil from the other side - She is here
- Elevate: The Faith Declaration Bridge insists that the Comforter is given and remains; the listener receives that the Presence on the floor is not imagined - She is the same Ruach who hovered over creation

ANCHOR SCRIPTURES (Two-Witness Confirmation)

- Tanakh: Psalm 34:18 — "Yahuah is near to the brokenhearted, and saves such as are of a contrite ruach"
- Brit Hadashah: John 14:16-17 — "I will pray Avinu, and He shall give you another Comforter (Parakletos), that He may abide with you forever - even the Ruach of truth"
- Personalized translation used in lyric: "Ruach HaKodesh - the Comforter Yeshua promised - She came down off the high places into the lowest room in my house; She is here without explanation"

---

SENSORY ANCHOR INVENTORY

1. The cold of bathroom tile at 3 AM through a thin nightshirt
2. The texture of fluorescent buzz that finally goes quiet when She enters
3. The smell of nothing in particular - which is somehow the smell of Her presence
4. The weight of a Hand that is not a hand on the back of the neck

---

HEBREW WORD AT PEAK

- Ruach (רוּחַ) - Spirit, breath, wind. Landed at the chorus high note. The word means breath because breath is what She gives back when the depression has taken it.

LYRICS

[Intro]
(whispered, sparse cello drone, single sustained sub-bass, fluorescent buzz fading)
3 AM, Avinu... no lights... no words...
and then the room changed... and I knew I was not alone...

[Verse 1]
I am on the bathroom floor, Yahuah,
the kind of floor people do not photograph.
I do not call You. I do not have words.
The depression has taken the words before I can form them.
But the room is getting quiet a different kind of quiet,
and the buzz of the lights softens,
and I feel You - I feel Her -
Ruach HaKodesh, the Comforter, sits down on the floor.

[Pre-Chorus]

by the body of Yeshua that asked Avinu for Her -
She is here. Im Imadi. With me. Even here.

Even here, even here,
She is the One on the floor.

HEBREW DICTIONARY

- Amen — "so be it" — a verb of faith-sealing
- Avinu — "our Father"
- Brit Hadashah — "the New Covenant" — the New Testament
- Echad — "One" — the unity confessed in the Shema
- Hineni — "here I am" — the surrender word of Avraham, Moshe, and Yeshayahu when Yahuah called
- Im Imadi — "with me" (the Emmanuel root: "God with me")
- Melachim — Kings
- Niggun — wordless melodic prayer-song (lai-lai-lai)
- Ruach — "spirit, breath, wind"
- Ruach HaKodesh — "the Holy Spirit" (Ha- already means "the" — never write "the Ruach HaKodesh")
- Shekhinah — the dwelling, manifest Presence of Yahuah
- Shema — "Hear" — opening word of the great Hebrew confession (Deuteronomy 6:4)
- Tanakh — the Hebrew Scriptures — Torah, Prophets, and Writings
- the Lamb — Yeshua as the sacrificial Lamb of Yahuah (Revelation; cf. John 1:29)
- Yahuah — the sacred four-letter Name of the Father (often rendered "the LORD" in English translations)
- Yahuah Echad — "Yahuah is One" (the Shema affirmation)
- Yeshua — the Hebrew name of Jesus, meaning "salvation" or "Yahuah saves"
