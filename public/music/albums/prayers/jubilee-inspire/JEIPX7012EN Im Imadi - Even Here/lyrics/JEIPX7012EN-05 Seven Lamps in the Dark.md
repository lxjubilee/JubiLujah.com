SONG-LEVEL EMOTIONAL ARC

- Establish: The depressed eye cannot bear floodlights; the depressive fog is dense; the mind has forgotten what light feels like
- Escalate: Yahuah does not flood the room — He lights seven small flames, one at a time, the way recovery actually comes; each lamp a Spirit named in Isaiah 11
- Elevate: The Faith Declaration Bridge ignites the seventh flame as Or - light - inside the listener's marrow; the listener receives a mind that can see its way one step at a time, not all at once

SENSORY ANCHOR INVENTORY

1. The smell of olive oil burning slowly - the same oil that fed the menorah for centuries
2. The sound of a wick taking flame - small, certain, almost a sigh
3. The texture of gold catching low light - warm, not blinding
4. The visual of a room going from black to grey to amber, one flame at a time

HEBREW WORD AT PEAK

- Or (אוֹר) - light. Landed at the Faith Declaration Bridge. The first word spoken in creation. The word that turns the darkness from a noun into a place light is on its way to.
