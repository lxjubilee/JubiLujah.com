- Establish: The listener brings their own depression - the specific morning they could not get up, the specific lie that lives in their head, the specific fear they have not named in daylight

SENSORY ANCHOR INVENTORY

1. The texture of sheets that have not been changed in too many days
2. The visual of a hand on the edge of a bed - someone sitting there waiting, patient, not in a hurry
3. The sound of breath being kept by the One sitting on the edge of the bed
4. The six-second silence where the specific weakness is named (Personal Insertion Beat)

HEBREW WORD AT PEAK

- Hineni (הִנֵּנִי) - here I am. Landed at the Hineni verse, which IS the song's emotional core. Avraham's word, Moshe's word, Yeshayahu's word, now the listener's word, prayed from the bottom bunk.
