LYRICS

[Intro]
(whispered, sparse cello drone, distant rush of seraphim wings, seraphic Kadosh barely audible)
Parokhet... the veil...
the seraphim are singing on the other side...

[Verse 1]
I am standing at Your inner veil, Avinu,
and the song from the other side
is the same Kadosh I heard at Your gate,
but louder, deeper, and not from human throats.
Six-winged beings are crying it
to each other across Your throne,
and I do not know
if I have any business pressing closer.
Your fire is too close.
Your Name is too holy.

[Pre-Chorus]
But Your veil is torn, Avinu -
the day Yeshua bowed His head
the parokhet split from top to bottom,
and the blood of the Lamb on the lintel
makes my face safe to lift -
the seraphim still cover theirs,
but mine is uncovered.

[Chorus]
Where seraphim cannot look, Yahuah -
the burning ones cover their faces,
but the blood of Yeshua uncovered mine -
I am standing where they shield,
I am singing where they hush,
Your parokhet is torn, Your access is mine,
Kadosh, Kadosh, Kadosh -
You alone are worthy, Yahuah,
I am closer than the burning ones.

[Verse 2]
The seraphim cover their faces, Adonai,
with two of their six wings -
but the blood of Yeshua uncovered mine -
and I am closer than the burning ones.
This is the revelation
Your veil has been carrying since Calvary:
the parokhet did not just tear in the Temple,
the parokhet walked through me
the day Yeshua bowed His head -
I am the access, I am the singer, I am the seen face -
and I sing what they cover their wings to behold.

[Pre-Chorus]

[Verse 3 - Hineni]
(intimate, almost spoken, face lifted)
Hineni, Avinu - here I am, undone before You,
every covered face laid open and true.
I lift the face the blood uncovered.
I sing the song the seraphim cover their wings for.
I do not boast of standing here -
the boast belongs entirely to the Lamb.
But here is my testimony, Yahuah:
the seraphim are fire and they shield;
I am dust and I see -
and the only difference between us is the blood of Yeshua.

(Faith Declaration Bridge - finished-reality, present tense, full ensemble unhushed)

HEBREW DICTIONARY

- Aaronic Blessing — the priestly blessing of Numbers 6:24-26 (You bless me and keep me, You make Your face to shine upon me...)
- Adonai — "my Lord"
- Amen — "so be it" — a verb of faith-sealing
- Avinu — "our Father"
- Brit Hadashah — "the New Covenant" — the New Testament
- Calvary — the place of Yeshua's crucifixion — where the veil was torn
- Echad — "One" — the unity confessed in the Shema
- Elohim — "God" — the plural-of-majesty Name of the Creator
- Hineni — "here I am" — the surrender word of Avraham, Moshe, and Yeshayahu when Yahuah called
- Kadosh — "holy"
- Niggun — wordless melodic prayer-song (lai-lai-lai)
- Parokhet — the inner veil of the Temple, torn at Calvary (Matthew 27:51)
- Shekhinah — the dwelling, manifest Presence of Yahuah
- Shema — "Hear" — opening word of the great Hebrew confession (Deuteronomy 6:4)
- Tanakh — the Hebrew Scriptures — Torah, Prophets, and Writings
- the Lamb — Yeshua as the sacrificial Lamb of Yahuah (Revelation; cf. John 1:29)
- Yahuah — the sacred four-letter Name of the Father (often rendered "the LORD" in English translations)
- Yeshayahu — Isaiah
- Yeshua — the Hebrew name of Jesus, meaning "salvation" or "Yahuah saves"
