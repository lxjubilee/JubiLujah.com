SENSORY ANCHOR INVENTORY

1. The metallic clink of self-made crowns hitting the bronze altar grate
2. The smell of the altar fire mixed with the smoke of every "look-what-I-did" trophy curling upward
3. The texture of the listener's hand opening for the first time in years - the fingers uncramping from the grip on her own throne
4. The taste of relief - not regret - when the crown leaves the head

- Tanakh — the Hebrew Scriptures — Torah, Prophets, and Writings
- Tehillim — Psalms
- the Lamb — Yeshua as the sacrificial Lamb of Yahuah (Revelation; cf. John 1:29)
- Yahuah — the sacred four-letter Name of the Father (often rendered "the LORD" in English translations)
- Yeshua — the Hebrew name of Jesus, meaning "salvation" or "Yahuah saves"
- Yochanan — John
