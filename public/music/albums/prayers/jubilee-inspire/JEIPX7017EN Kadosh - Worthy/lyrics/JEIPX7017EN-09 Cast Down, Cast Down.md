- Establish: The listener stands before the Mercy Seat - the place where heaven and earth meet; she is no longer at the threshold, she is at the climax of the temple journey
- Escalate: The kavod begins to fall - not just light, weight; she watches the twenty-four elders cast their crowns and the seraphim cover their faces; she joins the casting
- Elevate: She is pressed flat by the kavod and casts every will, every crown, every wish; the Gethsemane voice (permitted only here per master manual) lands - "not my will but Yours" - she casts everything down and the loudest moment of the album is sung from the floor

SENSORY ANCHOR INVENTORY

1. The physical weight of kavod pressing the listener's body flat against the floor of the Holy of Holies
2. The sound of crowns hitting marble - twenty-four of them, then one more (hers)
3. The texture of her cheek against the cold mercy-seat stone, her face fully cast down
4. The taste of "not my will but Yours" on her tongue - the same tongue Yeshua spoke it with in Gethsemane

[Intro]
(whispered, sparse cello drone, harp arpeggios beginning slow, distant elder voices casting crowns)
Kavod... weighty glory...
falling now... cast down, cast down...

[Verse 1]
I am before Your Mercy Seat, Avinu,
and I cannot stand under the weight.
Your kavod is not light alone - it is mass,
it is pressure, it is glory I can feel
on my shoulders.
And the twenty-four elders
are falling and casting and singing
and I cannot tell their voices
from the seraphim above Your throne -
the whole heaven is on the floor
and the floor is louder than every throne.

[Pre-Chorus]

every crown of being-right,
every crown of being-strong,
every crown of being-the-one-I-thought-I-was.

I am clear to come this close -
and being this close
is being this low.

[Chorus]
Cast down, cast down, Yahuah -
Kavod, Kavod, Kavod,
Your weighty glory falling like rain that has mass -
I cast every crown,
I cast every will,
I cast every wish I held above Your Name -
Cast down, cast down,
You alone are worthy, Yahuah,
You alone are worthy of the floor.

that has been pressed here before me -
the elders, the prophets, the martyrs,

HEBREW DICTIONARY

- Aaronic Blessing — the priestly blessing of Numbers 6:24-26 (You bless me and keep me, You make Your face to shine upon me...)
- Adonai — "my Lord"
- Amen — "so be it" — a verb of faith-sealing
- Avinu — "our Father"
- Brit Hadashah — "the New Covenant" — the New Testament
- Calvary — the place of Yeshua's crucifixion — where the veil was torn
- Echad — "One" — the unity confessed in the Shema
- Hineni — "here I am" — the surrender word of Avraham, Moshe, and Yeshayahu when Yahuah called
- Kadosh — "holy"
- Kavod — weighty glory; the manifest weight of Yahuah's presence
- Niggun — wordless melodic prayer-song (lai-lai-lai)
- Shekhinah — the dwelling, manifest Presence of Yahuah
- Shema — "Hear" — opening word of the great Hebrew confession (Deuteronomy 6:4)
- Tanakh — the Hebrew Scriptures — Torah, Prophets, and Writings
- the Lamb — Yeshua as the sacrificial Lamb of Yahuah (Revelation; cf. John 1:29)
- Yahuah — the sacred four-letter Name of the Father (often rendered "the LORD" in English translations)
- Yeshayahu — Isaiah
- Yeshua — the Hebrew name of Jesus, meaning "salvation" or "Yahuah saves"
