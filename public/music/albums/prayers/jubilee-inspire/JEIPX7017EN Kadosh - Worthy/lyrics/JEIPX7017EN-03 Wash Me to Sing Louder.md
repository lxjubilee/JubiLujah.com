SONG-LEVEL EMOTIONAL ARC

- Establish: With the crowns gone, the listener notices her own throat - the instrument that has been carrying every careless word; the laver is not for the body alone but for the mouth that will carry the song
- Escalate: The waters of the laver remember Eden, remember the deep, remember every clean word ever spoken on earth; they wash the listener's throat into a louder song
- Elevate: The laver's surface becomes a mirror - the listener sees the face that will sing, and the face has been cleansed for choir-work, not for solo apology

ANCHOR SCRIPTURES (Two-Witness Confirmation)

- Tanakh: Tehillim (Psalm) 51:7 - "purge me with hyssop and I shall be clean; wash me and I shall be whiter than snow"
- Brit Hadashah: Ephesians 5:26 - "that He might sanctify and cleanse it with the washing of water by the Word"
- Personalized translation used in lyric: "wash the throat that will carry the song, Yahuah - whiter than snow, cleaner than first breath, ready to sing You louder than the careless words ever flowed"

---

SENSORY ANCHOR INVENTORY

1. The cool resonance of water inside the bronze laver bowl as the listener leans over it
2. The smell of clean water mixed with hyssop in the air
3. The texture of the listener's reflection on the still surface - face uncovered, throat exposed for cleansing
4. The taste of the water on the tongue itself - rinsing the mouth before the choir takes the line

- Tanakh — the Hebrew Scriptures — Torah, Prophets, and Writings
- Tehillim — Psalms
- the Lamb — Yeshua as the sacrificial Lamb of Yahuah (Revelation; cf. John 1:29)
- Yahuah — the sacred four-letter Name of the Father (often rendered "the LORD" in English translations)
- Yeshua — the Hebrew name of Jesus, meaning "salvation" or "Yahuah saves"
