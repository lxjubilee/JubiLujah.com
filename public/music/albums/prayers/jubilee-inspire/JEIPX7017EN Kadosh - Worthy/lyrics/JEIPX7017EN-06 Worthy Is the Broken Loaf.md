SONG-LEVEL EMOTIONAL ARC

- Establish: The listener arrives at the Table of the Presence where twelve loaves sit before Yahuah continually; she has come to eat - and she discovers she has come to adore-while-eating
- Escalate: Every loaf consumed traces back to Bethlehem (House of Bread); the broken loaf in her hand is the broken body of the Lamb, and the only honest posture is to worship the Bread while it nourishes
- Elevate: The bridge installs the album refrain "Worthy is the Lamb that was slain" - the line that returns on every remaining track

---

ANCHOR SCRIPTURES (Two-Witness Confirmation)

- Tanakh: Vayikra (Leviticus) 24:5-9 - "you shall take fine flour and bake twelve cakes... and set them in two rows on the pure table before Yahuah continually"
- Brit Hadashah: Hitgalut (Revelation) 5:12 - "Worthy is the Lamb that was slain to receive power and riches and wisdom and might and honor and glory and blessing"
- Personalized translation used in lyric: "the Bread on the Table is the Body of the Lamb - and the Lamb is worthy - so the Loaf I am eating is worthy of the song I am singing - communion as adoration, not as memorial"

---

SENSORY ANCHOR INVENTORY

1. The texture of fine-flour Lechem HaPanim still warm in the listener's hand
2. The smell of frankincense set beside the bread per Vayikra 24:7
3. The taste of the broken loaf - wheat and salt and the strange covenantal weight on the tongue
4. The hushed sound of the listener's chewing being slower than reverence requires - because adoration has overtaken the appetite

HEBREW WORD AT PEAK

- Lechem HaPanim (לֶחֶם הַפָּנִים) - bread of the Presence, bread of the Face. Landed on the chorus high note. The Bread that has always been before Yahuah's Face.
