SONG-LEVEL EMOTIONAL ARC

- Establish: A worshipper limps up to gates that should be closed and discovers the song is already playing inside - she did not start the song, she joins one
- Escalate: The wonder that the threshold is loud with the seraphic Kadosh and the joining voices behind her swell to match - she is not auditioning, she is being received
- Elevate: The Shema is sung in full in the bridge while the listener crosses the threshold; she steps in not asking for one thing - only echoing the song that has been going around the throne since before there was time

LYRICS

[Intro]
(whispered, sparse cello drone, single ney flute breath, far choir Kadosh barely audible underneath)
Yahuah... Avinu... I'm at the gate...
And the song... the song is already going...

[Verse 1]
I come up the road expecting silence, Avinu,
expecting Your gates to be hushed for the day.
But the hinges are humming
and the lamp-niches are burning
and the song that I thought I would start
was started before I was born.
Kadosh, Kadosh, Kadosh -
I am not the singer, I am the joining.

by the body of Yeshua on the tree -
I am not crashing Your courts,

Kadosh, Kadosh, Kadosh,
worthy at the threshold, Yahuah.

[Verse 2]

and the word is older than I am -
the word the seraphim sang
before there was a sun to rise on.
Kadosh, Kadosh, Kadosh -
the song was always You.

the love that does not bargain,
the song that does not negotiate,
the praise that does not flinch when the answer is delayed.

You are worthy of every breath I have left.
