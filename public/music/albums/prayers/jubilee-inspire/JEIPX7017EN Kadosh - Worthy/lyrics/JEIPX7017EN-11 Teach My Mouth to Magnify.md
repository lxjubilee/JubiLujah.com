SONG-LEVEL EMOTIONAL ARC

- Establish: The listener has just finished praising over the lives of her loved ones - and she turns to her own life with the only petition allowed on this album: a petition for more adoration
- Escalate: She names her praise-deficit; the days she did not sing Him; the months of silence; she confesses not sin alone but the song-lessness that allowed sin space
- Elevate: The Personal Insertion Beat: 6 silent seconds where she names her own praise-deficit aloud; then the chorus picks up over the confession, and she walks out of Station 11 with a song instead of a request

---

ANCHOR SCRIPTURES (Two-Witness Confirmation)

- Tanakh: Tehillim (Psalm) 34:1, 3 - "I will bless Yahuah at all times; His praise shall continually be in my mouth... O magnify Yahuah with me, and let us exalt His Name together"
- Brit Hadashah: Ivrim (Hebrews) 13:15 - "by Him therefore let us offer the sacrifice of praise to Elohim continually, that is, the fruit of our lips giving thanks to His Name"
- Personalized translation used in lyric: "the only petition I have left is a petition for a mouth that will not stop singing You - teach my mouth to magnify - the only thing I need is the only thing You wanted from me first"

---

SENSORY ANCHOR INVENTORY

1. The texture of the listener's own lips - dry from the days she did not use them to bless Him
2. The smell of the praise-altar where her sacrifice of song will be laid
3. The taste of "tehillah" on her tongue - the Hebrew word that means "praise that bubbles up like spring water"
4. The sound of her own silence in the six-second beat - the exposed inventory of her praise-deficit

---

HEBREW WORD AT PEAK

- Tehillah (תְּהִלָּה) - praise that bubbles up, the word the entire book of Tehillim is named for. Landed on the chorus high note.
