SONG-LEVEL EMOTIONAL ARC

- Establish: The just-priestly-intercessing believer — having carried others' names — now brings their own deepest sin, the one they have not said aloud to any human, the one the basin and the laver and the altar did not quite reach because the listener could not say the word
- Escalate: 1 John 1:9's word ALL — He is faithful and just to cleanse from ALL unrighteousness — and the ALL is doing all the work; the unspeakable sin is included
- Elevate: The Personal Insertion Beat — the listener finally says it aloud in private, between them and Yahuah, and the blood swallows it

---

SENSORY ANCHOR INVENTORY

1. The image of a sin that the listener has not said aloud to any human — kept private even from the closest friend
2. The texture of the word ALL in 1 John 1:9 — three letters that swallow oceans
3. The childlike honesty of asking small and specific — "this one, this specific one"
4. The six seconds where the listener finally says it aloud, alone, between them and Yahuah

---

HEBREW WORD AT PEAK

- Tanakh — the Hebrew Scriptures — Torah, Prophets, and Writings
- Tehillim — Psalms
- the Lamb — Yeshua as the sacrificial Lamb of Yahuah (Revelation; cf. John 1:29)

- Yeshua — the Hebrew name of Jesus, meaning "salvation" or "Yahuah saves"
