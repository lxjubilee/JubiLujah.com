SONG-LEVEL EMOTIONAL ARC

- Establish: The worker prays for daily bread and the answer arrives before the bread does — the answer is a Person, Yeshua, the Bread who was broken before any loaf was baked
- Escalate: The Lechem HaPanim on the Table of Showbread was always twelve loaves before twelve tribes — and the broken Bread on the cross was Yeshua before the loaf on the kitchen table; the order is locked
- Elevate: The worker stops asking the loaf to be enough; the Person is enough, and the Person sets the table the loaf sits on

---

ANCHOR SCRIPTURES (TWO-WITNESS CONFIRMATION)

- Tanakh: Exodus 16:4 — "I will rain bread from heaven for you... a day's portion for the day" (manna grammar — daily) + Leviticus 24:5-9 — twelve loaves of Lechem HaPanim before the Presence continually
- Brit Hadashah: John 6:35, 48-51 — "I am the bread of life... the bread that I will give for the life of the world is My flesh"
- Personalized translation used in lyric: "Yeshua, You said You are the Bread of Life — and so the bread on my table came after the Bread on the cross, and the Bread on the cross has a Name, and the Name is the answer before the loaf is the answer"

HEBREW DICTIONARY

- Amen — "so be it" — a verb of faith-sealing
- Avinu — "our Father"
- Brit Hadashah — "the New Covenant" — the New Testament
- Echad — "One" — the unity confessed in the Shema
- HaMashiach — "the Messiah" (Ha- is Hebrew "the")
- Hineni — "here I am" — the surrender word of Avraham, Moshe, and Yeshayahu when Yahuah called
- Lechem — "bread"
- Lechem HaPanim — "Bread of the Face / Bread of the Presence" (the Showbread)
- Mashiach — "Anointed One" / Messiah
- Niggun — wordless melodic prayer-song (lai-lai-lai)
- Shekhinah — the dwelling, manifest Presence of Yahuah
- Shema — "Hear" — opening word of the great Hebrew confession (Deuteronomy 6:4)
- Shewbread — the Bread of the Presence (Lechem HaPanim) on the golden table
- Tanakh — the Hebrew Scriptures — Torah, Prophets, and Writings
- the Lamb — Yeshua as the sacrificial Lamb of Yahuah (Revelation; cf. John 1:29)
- Yahuah — the sacred four-letter Name of the Father (often rendered "the LORD" in English translations)
- Yeshua — the Hebrew name of Jesus, meaning "salvation" or "Yahuah saves"
- Yochanan — John
