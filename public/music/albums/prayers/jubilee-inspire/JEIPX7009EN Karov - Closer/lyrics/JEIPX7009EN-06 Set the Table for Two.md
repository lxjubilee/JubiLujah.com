---

SONG-LEVEL EMOTIONAL ARC

- Establish: The seeker reaches the Table of Showbread; twelve loaves sit before the Presence, but the table feels ceremonial, distant, weekly
- Escalate: Yeshua pulls out the second chair; the loaves on the gold table become bread between Friends; the Bread of the Face is the face she came to see across the table set for two
- Elevate: Daily companionship redefined — the Table is not a sacrament looked at, it is supper shared; the King who knocks (Rev 3:20) is the King who has already opened the door from inside her chamber

---

ANCHOR SCRIPTURES (Two-Witness Confirmation)

- Tanakh: Psalm 23:5 — "You prepare a table before me in the presence of my enemies"
- Brit Hadashah: Revelation 3:20 — "Behold, I stand at the door and knock; if any one hears My voice and opens the door, I will come in to him and eat with him, and he with Me"
- Personalized translation used in lyric: "You prepared a table before me, Yeshua — and the Bread of the Face is the Face I come to see across it — I open the door and You come in to me, and I to You, and we eat"

---

SENSORY ANCHOR INVENTORY

1. The warm yeasted smell of fresh bread baked that morning, sitting in twelve loaves
2. The scrape of a chair pulled out by a Hand she cannot see but feels
3. The texture of bread torn between two pairs of hands — His and hers
4. The taste of conversation that lasts longer than the meal

---

HEBREW WORD AT PEAK

- Lechem HaPanim (לֶחֶם הַפָּנִים) — Bread of the Face, Bread of the Presence. Landed on chorus. The Hebrew names the literal — the Bread literally faces Yahuah and Yahuah literally faces the Bread; the table is where the Faces meet.

[Intro]

[Verse 1]
I come to the Table, Yahuah,
expecting a ceremony, a memorial, a remembrance.
Twelve loaves, gold table, holy bread —
something to look at, not to share.
But the second chair was pulled out;
the Bread was already broken in half;
and the One sitting across the table
was the One I had been writing letters to.

You are sitting across from me.

draw me close, I am already drawn,
at Your Table, Yahuah.

HEBREW DICTIONARY

- Amen — "so be it" — a verb of faith-sealing
- Brit Hadashah — "the New Covenant" — the New Testament
- Echad — "One" — the unity confessed in the Shema
- Hineni — "here I am" — the surrender word of Avraham, Moshe, and Yeshayahu when Yahuah called
- Karov — "near, close"
- Lechem — "bread"
- Lechem HaPanim — "Bread of the Face / Bread of the Presence" (the Showbread)
- Niggun — wordless melodic prayer-song (lai-lai-lai)
- Shekhinah — the dwelling, manifest Presence of Yahuah
- Shema — "Hear" — opening word of the great Hebrew confession (Deuteronomy 6:4)
- Shewbread — the Bread of the Presence (Lechem HaPanim) on the golden table
- Tanakh — the Hebrew Scriptures — Torah, Prophets, and Writings
- Tehillim — Psalms
- the Lamb — Yeshua as the sacrificial Lamb of Yahuah (Revelation; cf. John 1:29)
- Yahuah — the sacred four-letter Name of the Father (often rendered "the LORD" in English translations)
- Yeshua — the Hebrew name of Jesus, meaning "salvation" or "Yahuah saves"
