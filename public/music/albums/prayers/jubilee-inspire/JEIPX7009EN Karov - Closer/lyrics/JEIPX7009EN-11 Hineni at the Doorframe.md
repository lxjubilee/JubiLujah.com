SONG-LEVEL EMOTIONAL ARC

- Establish: The seeker stands at the doorframe of His chamber — not asking for healing, breakthrough, or provision — asking for Him; she names the specific distance she still keeps from Him
- Escalate: She brings the marriage that has gone cold to Him, the calling that has dimmed, the prayer life that has crusted over, the place in her heart she still keeps from Him — Personal Insertion Beat where the listener names her own distance
- Elevate: Jeremiah 29:13 + James 4:8 land as the album's anchor pairing — she searches with all her heart and is found; she draws near and is drawn near to; the finished-reality receipt

- Tanakh: Jeremiah 29:13 — "You will seek Me and find Me, when you search for Me with all your heart" (ALBUM ANCHOR 1)

---

SENSORY ANCHOR INVENTORY

1. The wood-grain of a doorframe under the palm of a hand pressed in surrender
2. The cool draft from the chamber on the other side, promising what is just past the threshold
3. The specific distance the seeker still keeps — the one she will whisper into the Personal Insertion Beat
4. The taste of "Hineni" on the tongue when it is the first word said in months that means it

---

HEBREW WORD AT PEAK

- Hineni (הִנֵּנִי) — here I am, undone, fully present, every chamber of the heart laid open. Landed at the Hineni verse and at the chorus refrain. The Hebrew names the surrender that the English softens — Hineni is what Avraham said, what Moshe said, what Yeshayahu said. The seeker says it here as the song's whole offering.
