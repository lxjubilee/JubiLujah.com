[Intro]

The nightlight is yellow on the carpet, Avinu,
and the carpet does not know what it is standing on -
the carpet is standing on light,
and the light is older than electricity.
This is the menorah You hid
in a one-dollar plug-in -
seven flames disguised as one,
and the dark cannot put them out.
