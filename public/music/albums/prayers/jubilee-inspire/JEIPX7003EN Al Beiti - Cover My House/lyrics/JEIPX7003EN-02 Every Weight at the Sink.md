- Establish: A parent at the kitchen sink scraping plates at 10pm, scraping more than plates - the day's sharp words, the short fuse with the seven-year-old, the door they slammed - laying it on the counter as on a bronze altar

- Elevate: The Faith Declaration Bridge - by Yeshua's blood the day's failure is consumed and the household is not held hostage to the parent's bad day

- Yochanan — John
