LYRICS

[Intro]
(brighter than any other intro in the album, still cello drone but a tone higher, ney flute breath rising, soft tof beginning the walk-out tread)
Avinu... I am walking back out through the gates...
The exhaustion is still here... but so is the next step...

[Verse 1]
I came in limping, Avinu, and I am walking out -
not running, not flying, just walking,
the same body, the same calendar, the same long road -
but Your verdict has overruled the other verdict in the heavenlies,
and my body is catching up to Your verdict.
The strength was always Yeshua's stride
inside the stride I thought was mine -
and I walk out stronger.

[Pre-Chorus]
And You heard me, Avinu,
1 John says if I ask according to Your will You hear me,
and if You hear me I have already received -
by the blood of the Lamb that does the deeper hearing,
by the body of Yeshua on the cross -
I have asked for the next step
and the next step has been given.

[Chorus]
I walk out stronger, Avinu,
Your gate is still swinging, Your strength is still walking -
one more step, one more breath,
one more step, one more breath -
as my days, so shall Your strength be in me,
Your strength is Your stride inside my stride,
I walk out stronger, Avinu,
in Your courts, Yahuah-Chazak.

[Verse 2]
I am walking back out into Monday morning,
into the shift, the appointment, the next conversation,
the rent that is still due, the prodigal that is still gone.

The Levites keep singing.
Your courts do not require carrying every day's strength at once -
Your courts require letting today's strength be enough for today,
and tomorrow's strength be tomorrow's gift.

[Verse 3 - Hineni]
Hineni, Avinu - here I am, undone before You,
every hidden room laid open and true.
The hour with You is closing and the day is beginning.
I have prayed the prayer, I have eaten Your bread,
I have been washed at Your laver, I have been pinned by Your Kavod,
I have carried the names, I have brought my own next step -
and I am walking out stronger.
Not because I am stronger, but because the One inside me has not stopped walking.

[Bridge]
(Faith Declaration Bridge - finished-reality, present tense, the album's seal)
By Your Word, I have already walked out stronger.
By Your stripes, my body is catching up to Your verdict.
By the blood of the Lamb, today's strength is given.
In Yeshua's Name, I have asked, I have received, I am walking, right now.

[Bridge]
(Niggun Bridge - Shema sung in FULL - voices layering, the album's closing confession)

[Refrain]
(THE SEAL - Chazak, chazak, v'nit-chazek - the synagogue cry at the end of every book of Torah, sealed over the next twenty-four hours)

be strong, be strong, and we will be strengthened -
the Torah portion is sealed, the prayer hour is sealed,
the next twenty-four hours are sealed in Your Word.
You bless me and keep me through the next mile,
You make Your face to shine upon me at every appointment,
You lift Your countenance upon me and give me shalom
through the next single step.

[Chorus]
(Final Chorus - full ensemble, full Shekhinah-bloom harmonies, weeping strings, congregational sing-along, key resolving to D major on the final cadence per master manual Section VI)

one more step, one more breath,
one more step, one more breath -
as my days, so shall Your strength be in me,
Your strength is Your stride inside my stride,

[Outro]
(Sealed Amen - sustained 8 seconds, harmonized thirds and fifths, final niggun curl, resolving major)
Amen... Amen... Amen...
(breath returning to silence)

[Outro]
(Whisper-of-Elijah - almost inaudible)
"As your days, so shall your strength be." - Devarim 33:25
