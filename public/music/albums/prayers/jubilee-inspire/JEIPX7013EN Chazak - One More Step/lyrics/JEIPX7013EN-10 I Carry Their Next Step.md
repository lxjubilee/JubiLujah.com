[Intro]
(whispered, sparse cello drone, low oud heartbeat)
Avinu... I am not just bringing me to the throne...
I am bringing their names... the ones whose arms have dropped...

[Verse 1]
I come to Your throne with names on my chest, Avinu,
not my name first, but the names of the ones who are mid-battle.
The friend who is one phone call from collapsing.
The mother whose son has not come home in nine years.
The widow who is doing this Tuesday alone.
The brother who lost the job he loved.
I carry their next step, Avinu,
because today they cannot carry their own.

by the body of Yeshua the great High Priest -
I carry their next step,
and the carrying is the love.

I carry their next step, Avinu,
in Your courts, Yahuah-Roi.
