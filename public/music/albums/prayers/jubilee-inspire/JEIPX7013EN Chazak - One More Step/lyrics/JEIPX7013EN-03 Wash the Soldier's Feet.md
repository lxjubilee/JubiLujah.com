SONG-LEVEL EMOTIONAL ARC

- Establish: The long-haul soldier comes to the laver with feet caked in road-dust - the dust of the months and years of walking
- Escalate: The water of the Word washes the lies - "quitting is faith," "you have already given enough," "you should not have to walk this far"
- Elevate: The Faith Declaration Bridge declares the soldier's feet are clean for the next mile; Yeshua's kneeling at the disciples' feet is the operative image - and the chorus completes the Promise-Name-Receipt arc by closing in finished-reality voice (You wash, the soldier walks)

ANCHOR SCRIPTURES (Two-Witness Confirmation)

- Tanakh: Isaiah 1:16 - "Wash yourselves, make yourselves clean; put away the evil of your doings"
- Brit Hadashah: John 13:5 - "He poured water into a basin, and began to wash the disciples' feet, and to wipe them with the towel wherewith He was girded"
- Personalized translation used in lyric: "You knelt at the feet of the men You knew would walk Your gospel into the world, Yeshua - kneel at mine, the road dust is heavy, the next mile is real - and because You have already knelt, the soldier already walks"

LYRICS

[Intro]
(whispered, sparse cello drone, soft water-bowl sound effect)
The basin is full, Avinu... the basin is warm...

[Verse 1]
I come to Your laver with mud on my heels, Avinu,
the mud of the months that did not let up.
The mud of the funeral I drove home from,
the mud of the night shift, the mud of the waiting,
the mud of the prayer the doctor did not answer the way I wanted.
The water of Your Word is cold and bright -
You, the Servant-King, are kneeling at my feet,
and the soldier is walking, Yeshua, the soldier is walking.

[Pre-Chorus]
You took the basin and the towel,
You knelt at the feet of men about to walk Your gospel -
by the blood of the Lamb that does the deeper washing,
by the body of Yeshua on the cross -
the water washes the lies
that quitting is faith and tiredness is failure.

Your mayim chayim is alive,
in Your courts, Yahuah-Mekaddesh.
