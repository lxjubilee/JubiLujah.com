- Establish: Eliyahu collapses under the juniper tree saying "it is enough, take my life, I am no better than my fathers" - and an angel touches him with bread
- Escalate: The angel touched twice because the first touch was not enough; Yeshua does not begrudge the second touch; the listener has needed the second touch many times
- Elevate: The Faith Declaration Bridge declares Yeshua as the bread that fortified Eliyahu for forty days; the listener eats and walks the next mile in the strength of that meal

- Tanakh: 1 Kings 19:7-8 - "The angel of Yahuah came again the second time, and touched him, and said, Arise and eat; because the journey is too great for you. And he arose, and did eat and drink, and went in the strength of that meat forty days and forty nights"

- Personalized translation used in lyric: "The journey is too great for me, Avinu - arise and eat, the angel said - and the Bread is Yeshua and the strength is for the next mile, not for forty days at a time but for the one mile in front of me"
