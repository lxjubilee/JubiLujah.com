- Establish: The listener stands before the Mercy Seat carrying the weight of the long obedience - and the weight is real
- Escalate: The Kavod begins to fall - not light alone, but weight; the listener begins to be pressed to the floor by glory heavier than the burden
- Elevate: The Faith Declaration Bridge declares the only rest the long-walker ever gets is the rest of being pinned to the floor by something heavier than the burden; the Gethsemane voice is permitted here (the only station it is permitted) - "not my will, Avinu, but Yours"

- Tanakh: Exodus 33:14 - "My Presence shall go with you, and I will give you rest"
- Brit Hadashah: Matthew 26:39 (Yeshua at Gethsemane) - "O My Father, if it be possible, let this cup pass from Me; nevertheless not as I will, but as You will" - YESHUA-QUOTED LINE PLACEMENT

SENSORY ANCHOR INVENTORY

1. The exact pound-weight of the Kavod falling on the back of a worshipper
2. The smell of the inside of the Mercy Seat where the cherubim's wings meet
3. The texture of being pinned to the floor by glory and discovering the pinning is the rest
4. The sound of a cup being set down at Gethsemane after the choice has been made

---

HEBREW WORD AT PEAK

- Kavod (כָּבוֹד) - weighty glory. Landed at chorus climax. The Hebrew word means weight, not light - and the album's revelation about strength climaxes here.

LYRICS

[Intro]
(whispered, sparse cello drone, deep low cello sustained, almost no movement)
Avinu... I am before the Mercy Seat...
And the Kavod is starting to fall...

[Verse 1]
I come before Your Mercy Seat with the weight on my back, Avinu,
the weight of the long obedience, the long waiting, the long walk.
I had pictured Your Mercy Seat as a place of lightness,
a place where the weight would lift off
and I would float home.
But Your Kavod starts to fall,
and Your Kavod is heavier than my weight,
and the heavier weight presses me to the floor.

[Pre-Chorus]

heavier than the diagnosis, heavier than the years,
by the blood of the Lamb on the Mercy Seat that has spoken better things,
by the body of Yeshua on the cross -
Your Presence is rest because Your Presence has mass,
and the mass pins me down and the pinning is the rest.

[Chorus]

heavier than the weight, Avinu,
in Your Holy of Holies, Yahuah-Kavod.
