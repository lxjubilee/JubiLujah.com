SONG-LEVEL EMOTIONAL ARC

- Establish: The listener crosses the first veil into the Holy Place; the light changes from sun to lamplight; the darkness becomes hushed not horrifying; the wind one feels is not draft, it is breath
- Escalate: The recognition that Ruach HaKodesh is at home in the deep dark — She hovered over the face of the deep before Yahuah spoke "let there be light"; She is hovering over this listener's pre-dawn now
- Elevate: The promise — the same Voice that broke the first morning is about to break this one; the Spirit's hovering is itself the guarantee that the boker is en route

ANCHOR SCRIPTURES (TWO-WITNESS CONFIRMATION)

- Tanakh: Genesis 1:2 — "and the earth was without form, and void; and darkness was upon the face of the deep. And the Ruach Elohim moved upon the face of the waters"
- Brit Hadashah: Romans 8:26 — "likewise the Ruach also helps our weaknesses: for we know not what we should pray for as we ought: but the Ruach Herself makes intercession for us with groanings which cannot be uttered"
- Personalized translation used in lyric: "the Ruach Kodesh hovers over the darkness on the face of my deep, Avinu — She has been here the whole night — She is not afraid of the dark — She is at home in it — She is praying through me with groanings I cannot put into words"

---

SENSORY ANCHOR INVENTORY

1. The sensation of a presence in a dark room that is not threatening — warmth without source
2. The barely-audible breath sound at the edge of hearing, like wings folding
3. The sight of a single lamp flame standing perfectly still though no glass surrounds it
4. The feeling of being prayed for without praying — the groan happening in the chest

HEBREW DICTIONARY

- Avinu — "our Father"
- Boker — "morning"
- Brit Hadashah — "the New Covenant" — the New Testament
- Echad — "One" — the unity confessed in the Shema
- Elohim — "God" — the plural-of-majesty Name of the Creator
- Hineni — "here I am" — the surrender word of Avraham, Moshe, and Yeshayahu when Yahuah called
- Niggun — wordless melodic prayer-song (lai-lai-lai)
- Ruach — "spirit, breath, wind"
- Ruach HaKodesh — "the Holy Spirit" (Ha- already means "the" — never write "the Ruach HaKodesh")

- Shekhinah — the dwelling, manifest Presence of Yahuah
- Shema — "Hear" — opening word of the great Hebrew confession (Deuteronomy 6:4)
- Tanakh — the Hebrew Scriptures — Torah, Prophets, and Writings
- the Lamb — Yeshua as the sacrificial Lamb of Yahuah (Revelation; cf. John 1:29)
- Yahuah — the sacred four-letter Name of the Father (often rendered "the LORD" in English translations)
- Yeshua — the Hebrew name of Jesus, meaning "salvation" or "Yahuah saves"
