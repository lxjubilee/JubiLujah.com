SONG-LEVEL EMOTIONAL ARC

- Establish: The listener stands before the Menorah in the windowless Holy Place; outside it is still dark; the only light in the room is the seven flames
- Escalate: The discovery that the listener does not need sunrise yet — only the next step; and the Menorah's seven flames are exactly enough light for one more step; teshuvah breath — forgive me for despising the small light
- Elevate: The Faith Declaration Bridge: His Word is a lamp to the feet, not a floodlight to the future; the receipt beat lands on Or — the listener has already received the light for the next step

4. The warmth on the face of someone leaning close enough to a small flame to feel the heat
