SONG-LEVEL EMOTIONAL ARC

- Establish: The listener arrives at the table of Shewbread weary, hungry, despairing — like Eliyahu under the broom tree saying "it is enough, take my life"
- Escalate: The angel does not bring a sermon, the angel brings bread — and the Lechem HaPanim is the bread that was prepared for exactly this journey
- Elevate: Yeshua is the Bread of Life — He was baked in Beit Lechem (House of Bread) for exactly this hour, exactly this listener, exactly this long night; the receipt beat is the active eaten Bread, not passive feeding

---

ANCHOR SCRIPTURES (TWO-WITNESS CONFIRMATION)

- Tanakh: 1 Kings 19:7-8 — the angel feeding Eliyahu under the broom tree: "the journey is too great for thee. And he arose, and did eat and drink, and went in the strength of that meat forty days and forty nights"
- Brit Hadashah: John 6:35 — "I am the Bread of Life: he that comes to Me shall never hunger; and he that believes on Me shall never thirst"
- Personalized translation used in lyric: "the journey was too great for me, Avinu, and Yeshua came to me at the broom tree with Bread — He is the Lechem HaPanim of the Presence, He is the Bread of Life — He was baked in Beit Lechem for the long night I am walking"

---

SENSORY ANCHOR INVENTORY

1. The smell of warm bread in a room that has been cold for hours
2. The texture of breaking a soft loaf with hands too tired to hold a knife
3. The taste of bread eaten when the body had forgotten it was hungry
4. The sound of a chair being pulled out at a table where the seat has the listener's name
