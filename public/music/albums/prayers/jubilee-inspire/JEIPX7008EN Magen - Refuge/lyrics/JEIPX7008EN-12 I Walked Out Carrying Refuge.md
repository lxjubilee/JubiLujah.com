- Establish: The believer turns from the Mercy Seat and starts the walk back out the gates - same highway, same news cycle, same small fears waiting at the curb
- Escalate: But the covering is on now - the Magen has been put on like a garment, the Refuge is wearing the believer the way the believer is wearing the Refuge
- Elevate: The Shema is sung in full in the bridge - the believer walks back into the world confessing Echad, carrying the Magen, breathing shalom in a body that was breathing fear at the gate one hour ago

SENSORY ANCHOR INVENTORY

1. The visual of Temple gates closing behind a body that is no longer the body that came in
2. The smell of dawn on stone after a night of weeping
3. The sound of one set of footsteps walking out steady where one set walked in trembling
4. The texture of a covering settled around the shoulders that was not there one hour ago
