LYRICS

[Intro]
(whispered, cello drone, faint water-trickle sample)
Yahuah... the laver is full...
And I am dusty with what chased me here...

[Verse 1]
I come to Your laver, Yahuah,
with a layer of fear cured into my skin
the way the leather hardens on a saddle
that has not been off the horse in months.
My shoulder is up. My ear is cocked.
I have been listening for the wrong sound
for so many years
that I have forgotten what Your voice sounds like in a quiet room.

[Pre-Chorus]
Wash me by the Word, Avinu -
wash me by the blood of Yeshua -
wash off what chased me to the gate;
I do not want to carry it in.

[Chorus]
Wash off what chased me, Yahuah -
the cortisol, the cocked ear, the cracked sleep -
Mayim Chayim - Your living waters move -
Mayim Chayim, rinse me back.
You wash me by the body of Yeshua,
You wash me by the Word that was breathed,
under Your wing, under Your wing,
the fear is rinsed, I am held.

[Verse 2]
Your laver remembers Eden, Avinu -
the garden before the snake had teeth,
the walk in the cool of the day,
the man who slept without one door locked.
Bring my body back to that man.
Bring my body back to that woman.
Bring my body back to the body
that did not flinch when You came near.

[Pre-Chorus]
Wash me deeper, Avinu -
Your Eden river is moving under the basin -
wash off the residue I learned to live with;
I do not carry it past this water.

[Chorus]
Wash off what chased me, Yahuah -
the cortisol, the cocked ear, the cracked sleep -
Mayim Chayim - Your living waters move -
Mayim Chayim, rinse me back.
You wash me by the body of Yeshua,
You wash me by the Word that was breathed,
under Your wing, under Your wing,
the fear is rinsed, I am held.

[Instrumental]
(violin niggun, ornamented, water-trickle sample audible, ney flute response - 30 seconds)

[Verse 3 - Hineni]
Hineni, Avinu - here I am, undone before You,
every hidden room laid open and true.
Wash the hallway where I taught myself to walk listening;
wash the kitchen where I learned to keep a knife close;
wash the bedroom where I learned to sleep half-awake.
Wash the body that learned to live like prey
and teach it - one Mayim Chayim drop at a time -
that You, the Shepherd, do not need Your sheep to stay armed.

[Bridge]
(Faith Declaration Bridge - finished-reality, present tense)
By Your Word, it is done.
By Your stripes, I am washed clean.
By the blood of the Lamb, the fear has no skin to cling to.
In Yeshua's Name, perfect love has cast it out - I am washed, I am held.

[Bridge]
(Niggun Bridge - Shema melodic fragment hummed within lai-lai-lai)
Lai-lai-lai... lai-lai-lai...
(Shema melody contour hummed, Echad held)
Mayim Chayim... lai-lai-lai...
Yahuah Echad... lai-lai-lai...

[Refrain]
(Psalm 23 personalized)
Though I walk through the valley of the shadow of death,
I fear no evil, Yahuah,
for You are with me;
Your rod and Your staff, they comfort me -
and Your laver washes the valley off my feet.

[Chorus]
(Final chorus, full ensemble, single high harmony layered)
Wash off what chased me, Yahuah -
the cortisol, the cocked ear, the cracked sleep -
Mayim Chayim - the living waters move -
Mayim Chayim, rinse me back.
Wash me by the body of Yeshua,
wash me by the Word that was breathed,
under the wing, under the wing,
the fear is rinsed, the body is held.

[Outro]

(breath returning to silence, water-trickle fades)

[Outro]
(Whisper-of-Elijah - almost inaudible)
"There is no fear in love; but perfect love casts out fear." - 1 Yochanan 4:18

HEBREW DICTIONARY

- Amen — "so be it" — a verb of faith-sealing
- Avinu — "our Father"
- Brit Hadashah — "the New Covenant" — the New Testament
- Echad — "One" — the unity confessed in the Shema
- Eden — the garden where Yahuah walked with humanity in the cool of the day
- Hineni — "here I am" — the surrender word of Avraham, Moshe, and Yeshayahu when Yahuah called
- Magen — "shield"
- Mayim Chayim — "living water" (cf. John 4:10)
- Niggun — wordless melodic prayer-song (lai-lai-lai)
- Shema — "Hear" — opening word of the great Hebrew confession (Deuteronomy 6:4)
- Tanakh — the Hebrew Scriptures — Torah, Prophets, and Writings
- the Lamb — Yeshua as the sacrificial Lamb of Yahuah (Revelation; cf. John 1:29)
- Yahuah — the sacred four-letter Name of the Father (often rendered "the LORD" in English translations)
- Yahuah Echad — "Yahuah is One" (the Shema affirmation)
- Yeshua — the Hebrew name of Jesus, meaning "salvation" or "Yahuah saves"
- Yochanan — John
