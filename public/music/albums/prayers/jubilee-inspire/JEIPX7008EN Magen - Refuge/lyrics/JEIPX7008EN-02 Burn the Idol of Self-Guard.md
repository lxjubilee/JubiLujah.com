LYRICS

[Intro]
(whispered, cello drone, ember-crackle sample low in the mix)
Yahuah... I built a chariot...
And I called it safety...

[Verse 1]
I trusted chariots, Yahuah,
horses and alarm systems and a second password.
I trusted the deadbolt
more than I trusted the Name on the door.
And the chariot got heavier every year
until I could not lift my head to look at You,
because my head was bowed to a god
I had welded out of fear.

[Pre-Chorus]
Teshuvah, Yahuah - I am turning,
turning back, turning home.
Your bronze fire is hot, my hands are open -
take what should not be in them.

[Chorus]
I lay it down, let it burn -
the chariot of self-guard, I lay it down, let it burn.
By the blood of the Lamb, I lay it down,
by the body of Yeshua on the cross, let it burn.
I trust Your Name above the door,
not the lock I bought with my own fear -
the chariot has burned, Your Name remains;
Yahuah-Magen, the guard is Yours.

[Verse 2]
Here is the alarm code I memorized
the year I stopped sleeping, Yahuah -
here is the second password I built
in case Your Name was not enough.
Here is the plan B drawer.
Here is the perimeter I walked at midnight
counting windows, checking the locks twice -
take the watch back, Avinu;
I lay down the small gods of fear.

[Pre-Chorus]
Teshuvah, Yahuah - deeper, deeper -
I am turning back, turning home, turning all the way in.
Your bronze fire is hot, my hands are emptied -
take what should not be in them.

[Chorus]
I lay it down, let it burn -
the chariot of self-guard, I lay it down, let it burn.
By the blood of the Lamb, I lay it down,
by the body of Yeshua on the cross, let it burn.
I trust Your Name above the door,
not the lock I bought with my own fear -
the chariot has burned, Your Name remains;
Yahuah-Magen, the guard is Yours.

[Instrumental]
(violin niggun, mournful and ornamented, ney flute response, low cello drone - 30 seconds, ember crackle continues)

[Verse 3 - Hineni]
Hineni, Avinu - here I am, undone before You,
every hidden room laid open and true.
Every chariot in the garage,
every plan B in the drawer,
every door code I memorized
so I would not have to memorize Your Name -
I climb onto the altar, Yahuah,
because You did not say "send Your idols up."
You said: present your bodies.
So my body comes too. Let it burn what should burn.

[Bridge]
(Faith Declaration Bridge - finished-reality, present tense, rising)
By Your Word, it is done.
By Your stripes, I am freed of the false guard.
By the blood of the Lamb, I have access without a second lock.
In Yeshua's Name, the chariot has burned and the Name on the door is enough.

[Bridge]

Yahuah Echad... lai-lai-lai...

[Refrain]

Some trust in chariots, some in horses,
but I remember Your Name, Yahuah,
I remember Your Name, Yahuah,

[Chorus]

I lay it down, let it burn -
the chariot of self-guard, I lay it down, let it burn.

not the lock I bought with my own fear -
