SONG-LEVEL EMOTIONAL ARC

- Establish: The believer arrives at the Mercy Seat and feels the kavod-weight begin to fall - not as terror but as gathering
- Escalate: Yeshua's wing-lament over Yerushalayim is sung back to Him as the believer's own request: "gather me too, the way You wanted to gather them"
- Elevate: Kavod and Kanaph together - the weight is wing; the wing is wing of the One who is Echad; the believer is undone by being gathered

ANCHOR SCRIPTURES (Two-Witness Confirmation)

- Tanakh: Psalm 91:1-4 (ALBUM ANCHOR 1) - "He who dwells in the secret place of El Elyon shall abide under the shadow of Shaddai... He shall cover you with His pinions, and under His wings you shall trust"
- Brit Hadashah: Matthew 23:37 (YESHUA-QUOTED LINE) - "how often I wanted to gather your children together, the way a hen gathers her chicks under her wings"
- Personalized translation used in lyric: "I dwell in the secret place of El Elyon, Yahuah - I abide under the shadow of Shaddai - You cover me with Your pinions, under Your wings I trust - and Yeshua, You said You wanted to gather them - gather me too tonight"

SENSORY ANCHOR INVENTORY

1. The weight of feathers heavier than fur settling over the shoulders
2. The visual of a hen's wings dropping suddenly low over chicks in a yard
3. The sound of breath warming under a covering at close range
4. The texture of the hem of a garment touched in faith, the same hem that is the wing
