SONG-LEVEL EMOTIONAL ARC

- Establish: The believer stands before the seven-branched menorah and asks for the light to travel - the road needs it, the doorway needs it, the long drive needs it
- Escalate: The seven Spirits of Yahuah do not stay in the Holy Place - they walk out with the believer the way the pillar of fire walked with the camp
- Elevate: Or - light - declared in finished-reality voice; the dark has been given borders; the watch-fires are lit around the perimeter of the believer's life
