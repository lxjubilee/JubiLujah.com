- Song Length: 5:00 (Prayer Song override of 3:30-4:00)

---

SONG-LEVEL EMOTIONAL ARC

- Establish: The wounded one stands before the Mercy Seat — having walked through the now-portable veil — and asks Moshe's prayer: show me Your Kavod
- Escalate: The Kavod is not only light — it is weight; it falls like rain that has mass; the body that has been carrying its diagnosis finally has something heavier pressing it to the floor
- Elevate: Total surrender — the YESHUA-QUOTED line from John 17:24 personalized: "let me behold Your kavod, Yeshua — I am one of the them"

---

ANCHOR SCRIPTURES (TWO-WITNESS CONFIRMATION)

- Tanakh: Exodus 33:18 — Moshe prayed "show me Your kavod"
- Brit Hadashah: John 17:24 — Yeshua's High-Priestly Prayer — "Father, I will that they also, whom You have given Me, be with Me where I am; that they may behold My kavod"
- Personalized translation used in lyric: "show me Your Kavod, Yahuah — and Yeshua, in Your High-Priestly Prayer You asked the Father that the ones the Father gave You would behold Your Kavod — and I am one of the them, so let me see what You prayed I would see"

---

SENSORY ANCHOR INVENTORY

1. The weight of light — Kavod as mass, not just brightness
2. The temperature of a Presence that fills the room before the eyes adjust
3. The sound of crowns being cast (Revelation 4:10) — small metallic, then larger, then everything
4. The sensation of the body finally being heavier-with-Glory than heavy-with-diagnosis

---

HEBREW WORD AT PEAK

- Kavod (כָּבוֹד) — weighty glory. Landed at the chorus climax (the album's highest note overall).
