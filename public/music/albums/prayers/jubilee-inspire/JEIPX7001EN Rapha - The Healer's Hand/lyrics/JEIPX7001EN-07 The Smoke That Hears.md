[Intro]
(whispered, cello drone, faint frankincense-coal crackle sample)
Your altar is small, Avinu...

[Verse 1]
I come to Your Incense Altar with a prayer I cannot finish, Yahuah —
the kind of prayer that catches in my throat
the way a swallow catches when the body is afraid.
You said let my prayer be set before You as incense —
even when the prayer is half a sentence,
even when the prayer is only a breath,
even when the prayer is only my body
saying the part my mouth cannot say.

[Pre-Chorus]
And the smoke is older than the lung that breathes it.
The smoke does not need a full sentence —
the smoke finishes the sentence for me,
rising in a column that is older than my voice,
older than my body,
older than my disease.

incense before Your Face, before Your Throne —
I lay it down, I am made whole,
the smoke knows my name when my mouth forgets.

when I am too tired to add my own.

HEBREW DICTIONARY

- Amen — "so be it" — a verb of faith-sealing
- Avinu — "our Father"
- Brit Hadashah — "the New Covenant" — the New Testament
- Echad — "One" — the unity confessed in the Shema
- Hineni — "here I am" — the surrender word of Avraham, Moshe, and Yeshayahu when Yahuah called
- Ketoret — "incense" — the prayer-aroma offering
- Niggun — wordless melodic prayer-song (lai-lai-lai)
- Rapha — "Healer; the One who knits, mends, restores"
- Ruach — "spirit, breath, wind"
- Ruach HaKodesh — "the Holy Spirit" (Ha- already means "the" — never write "the Ruach HaKodesh")
- Ruach Kodesh — alternative form: "the Spirit, Holy"
- Shema — "Hear" — opening word of the great Hebrew confession (Deuteronomy 6:4)
- Tanakh — the Hebrew Scriptures — Torah, Prophets, and Writings
- the Lamb — Yeshua as the sacrificial Lamb of Yahuah (Revelation; cf. John 1:29)
- Yahuah — the sacred four-letter Name of the Father (often rendered "the LORD" in English translations)
- Yeshua — the Hebrew name of Jesus, meaning "salvation" or "Yahuah saves"
