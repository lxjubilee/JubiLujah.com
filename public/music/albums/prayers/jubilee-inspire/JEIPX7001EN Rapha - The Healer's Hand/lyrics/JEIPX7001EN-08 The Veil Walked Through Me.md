SONG-LEVEL EMOTIONAL ARC

- Establish: The wounded one stands at the inner veil — the Parokhet — and recalls that this is the veil that was torn from top to bottom at Yeshua's death
- Escalate: The cinematic revelation — the veil did not stay in the Temple after it tore; the veil walked itself through the chest of every believer that day; the listener IS the access now
- Elevate: Healing flows because the access has moved — from a place to a Person, and the Person is walking through the person

---

ANCHOR SCRIPTURES (TWO-WITNESS CONFIRMATION)

- Tanakh: Exodus 26:33 — "the veil shall divide unto you between the Holy Place and the Most Holy"
- Brit Hadashah: Matthew 27:51 (+ Hebrews 10:19–20) — "the veil of the Temple was torn in two from the top to the bottom"; "we have boldness to enter into the Most Holy by the blood of Yeshua, by a new and living way which He has consecrated for us, through the veil, that is to say, His flesh"
- Personalized translation used in lyric: "the veil was torn the day Yeshua bowed His head — and the tearing did not stop in the Temple; the veil walked itself through my chest, and I am the access now because the access is a Person, and the Person walked Himself through me"

---

SENSORY ANCHOR INVENTORY

1. The sound of fabric tearing — slow, from the top, heavy
2. The visual of a thick curtain split in two, light pouring through
3. The sensation of something walking through the chest the way water walks through a wound
4. The weight of standing where only the High Priest stood, and finding the room familiar

HEBREW DICTIONARY

- Aharon — Aaron — the first High Priest, who carried twelve names on his breastplate
- Amen — "so be it" — a verb of faith-sealing
- Avinu — "our Father"
- Brit Hadashah — "the New Covenant" — the New Testament
- Echad — "One" — the unity confessed in the Shema
- Elohim — "God" — the plural-of-majesty Name of the Creator
- HaMashiach — "the Messiah" (Ha- is Hebrew "the")
- Hineni — "here I am" — the surrender word of Avraham, Moshe, and Yeshayahu when Yahuah called
- Mashiach — "Anointed One" / Messiah
- Niggun — wordless melodic prayer-song (lai-lai-lai)
- Or — "light"
- Parokhet — the inner veil of the Temple, torn at Calvary (Matthew 27:51)
- Rapha — "Healer; the One who knits, mends, restores"
- Ruach HaKodesh — "the Holy Spirit" (Ha- already means "the" — never write "the Ruach HaKodesh")
- Shekhinah — the dwelling, manifest Presence of Yahuah
- Shema — "Hear" — opening word of the great Hebrew confession (Deuteronomy 6:4)
- Tanakh — the Hebrew Scriptures — Torah, Prophets, and Writings
- the Lamb — Yeshua as the sacrificial Lamb of Yahuah (Revelation; cf. John 1:29)
- Yahuah — the sacred four-letter Name of the Father (often rendered "the LORD" in English translations)
- Yeshayahu — Isaiah
- Yeshua — the Hebrew name of Jesus, meaning "salvation" or "Yahuah saves"
