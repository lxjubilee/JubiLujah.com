SONG-LEVEL EMOTIONAL ARC

- Establish: The listener, prostrate from Station 9, rises and turns to carry other names into the Holy Place; she takes Aharon's posture and recognizes she has been carrying not just names but scripts
- Escalate: The breastplate metaphor expanded - Aharon carried tribes, not timelines; the listener has been smuggling her plans for other people across the threshold along with their names; tonight she carries the name and releases the plan
- Elevate: Personal Insertion Beat where the listener speaks BOTH the name AND her plan-for-them aloud and releases both into the Hand

ANCHOR SCRIPTURES (Two-Witness Confirmation)

- Tanakh: Exodus 28:29 - "Aharon shall bear the names of the sons of Yisrael in the breastplate of judgment upon his heart, when he goes in to the Holy Place, for a memorial before Yahuah continually"
- Brit Hadashah: Philippians 4:6 + 1 Peter 5:7 - "Be careful for nothing; but in every thing by prayer and supplication with thanksgiving let your requests be made known unto Elohim" / "casting all your care upon Him; for He cares for you"
- Personalized translation used in lyric: "I am a priest now (1 Kefa 2:9) - I wear the breastplate Aharon wore - I carry the names of those I love into the Holy Place on my heart - and I leave my plan for them on the floor at the threshold, because the breastplate held names, not scripts"

SENSORY ANCHOR INVENTORY

1. The weight of twelve gemstones sewn into linen over the heart, and the second sensation of the OTHER hand opening palm-up beside it
2. The specific weight of intercession-with-plan vs. intercession-without-plan - felt in the shoulders
3. The six seconds of held silence where the listener speaks both the name AND the plan-for-them aloud, releasing both
4. The visual of bodies and stories being carried - not just metaphorically - into the Holy Place
