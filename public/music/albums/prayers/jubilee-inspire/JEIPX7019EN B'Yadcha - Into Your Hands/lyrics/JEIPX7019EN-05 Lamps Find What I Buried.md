- Establish: The listener stands before the seven flames of the menorah; her hands are open, but she suspects she has buried things she did not bring to the altar
- Escalate: The seven Spirits as illuminators, not accusers; the light is the way the listener finally finds the things she has been pretending she already let go of
- Elevate: Faith Declaration Bridge - Or shines, what was hidden is exposed and released into His hand; the buried thing is unburied so it can be surrendered
