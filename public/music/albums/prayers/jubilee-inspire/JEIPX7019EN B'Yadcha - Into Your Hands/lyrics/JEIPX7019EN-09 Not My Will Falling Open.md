SENSORY ANCHOR INVENTORY

1. The visual of the Mercy Seat with the two cherubim's wings touching above it, and the listener prostrate beneath
2. The weight of kavod that has mass - rain that presses down because it is heavy, not light
3. The feel of the listener's own hands falling open at her sides under the kavod, without her deciding to open them
4. The taste of the Gethsemane cup imagined on the listener's own tongue, and the swallow that means surrender
