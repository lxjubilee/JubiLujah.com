LYRICS

[Intro]
(whispered, sparse cello drone, faint water-trickle sample under)
Yahuah... Avinu... we have left the altar...
We are standing at the laver... with the smell of bronze still on our hands...

[Verse 1]
We have laid the nation down, Yahuah,
and now our hands are dark with what we laid down.
We cannot enter the next room dirty;
we cannot carry the offering's ash into the Holy Place.
You said You would sprinkle clean water on us;
You said You would give us a new heart;
You said You would put Your ruach within us.
You said it to a people, Avinu - not only to a person.

[Pre-Chorus]
Wash us together, Avinu, wash us together...
By the water and the Word and the blood of the Lamb,
by the side of Yeshua opened on the tree -
You have promised us this washing.

[Chorus]
Wash a people clean, Avinu -
let Your Mayim Chayim run through a nation -
Rapheh - heal our land,
Rapheh - hear from heaven.
A new heart for a people,
a new ruach for a land,
wash a people clean,
in the washing of Your Word.

[Verse 2]
Your laver remembers Eden, Avinu -
the first water that watered the garden,
the first commission that crowned the man and the woman,
the first shalom that walked in the cool of the day.
Wash our people back to that mandate.
Wash our hands of what we picked up from the wrong tree.
Wash our mouths of the words that cursed our neighbors.
Wash our country in the Mayim Chayim of Yeshua.

[Pre-Chorus]

[Verse 3 - Hineni]
(intimate, almost spoken)
Hineni, Avinu - here we are, undone before You,
every hidden room of this land laid open and true.
We do not wash our hands of our country;
we let our country be washed in our hands.
Wash the watchman first, Yahuah -
the watchman is not above the people.
Then send the watchman back to the wall
with clean hands and a soft heart.

[Bridge]
(Faith Declaration Bridge - finished-reality voice, rising)

and a new ruach You put within us;

and give us a heart of flesh -
together, as one, for the sake of the land.

[Chorus]
