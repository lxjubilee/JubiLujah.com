SONG-LEVEL EMOTIONAL ARC

- Establish: The listener stands at the Menorah and asks for the seven flames to be turned on the relationship — but expects the light to expose the other side's wrongs
- Escalate: Instead the seven Spirits illuminate her blind spot first — wisdom and understanding and counsel and might and knowledge and the fear of Yahuah and the Spirit of Yahuah Himself show the listener what she has been doing for years that she could not see
- Elevate: Faith Declaration Bridge — the same Light that exposed her blind spot also exposes the silent one's wound; both are visible in the same Light; the path of fellowship lights up between them (1 John 1:7)

ANCHOR SCRIPTURES (TWO-WITNESS CONFIRMATION)

- Tanakh: Isaiah 11:2 — "the Ruach of Yahuah shall rest upon Him — the Ruach of wisdom and understanding, the Ruach of counsel and might, the Ruach of knowledge and of the fear of Yahuah"; with Psalm 139:23-24 — "search me, O Elohim, and know my heart; try me, and know my thoughts; see if there be any wicked way in me, and lead me in the way everlasting"
- Brit Hadashah: 1 John 1:7 — "if we walk in the light, as He is in the light, we have fellowship one with another, and the blood of Yeshua His Son cleanses us from all sin"
- Personalized translation used in lyric: "Avinu, You said if I walk in the Light I have fellowship with her, and the blood of Yeshua cleanses us — search me first; turn the seven lamps on my blind spot before they turn on hers; let me see what I have been doing that I could not see"

SENSORY ANCHOR INVENTORY

1. Seven flames in a curved row, casting seven different angles of light on the same room
2. The shadow I have been standing in for years, suddenly lit from the side
3. The look on her face under the same Light — not the version I described, the version You see
4. The sound of "oh" — the breath of seeing what was true the whole time
