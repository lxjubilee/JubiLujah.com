SONG-LEVEL EMOTIONAL ARC

- Establish: The listener has come past the gate and stands at the Bronze Altar with both hands full of the rupture — but Yahuah will only accept her share, not the other side's share
- Escalate: The list of "my half" — the words I said, the silence I kept, the pride I would not put down, the seven-year grudge I called wisdom — laid on the altar one by one
- Elevate: Teshuvah lands at the Faith Declaration Bridge — the listener stops bargaining about whose fault it is and gives Yahuah her entire share; "my half of the fire" becomes the only sacrifice she has authority to give

---

ANCHOR SCRIPTURES (TWO-WITNESS CONFIRMATION)

- Tanakh: Psalm 51:17 — "the sacrifices of Elohim are a broken spirit — a broken and a contrite heart, O Elohim, You will not despise"
- Brit Hadashah: Matthew 5:23-24 — "if you bring your gift to the altar and there remember that your brother has something against you, leave there your gift before the altar, and go your way; first be reconciled to your brother, and then come and offer your gift"
- Personalized translation used in lyric: "Yeshua, You said leave the gift at the altar and go first to my brother — and I cannot reach my brother yet — so I am bringing my half of the fire to You first, and asking You to carry my repentance across the silence the way only Ruach HaKodesh can"

---

SENSORY ANCHOR INVENTORY

1. The heat of bronze that has been hot for a thousand years and is hot enough to take my share
2. The list of "my half" — the specific words, the specific silence, the specific pride — written on paper before the altar
3. The texture of letting go of the half that was never mine to carry — the other side's half, the part I cannot fix
4. The sound of my own voice saying "my fault" out loud for the first time

---

HEBREW WORD AT PEAK

- Teshuvah (תְּשׁוּבָה) — turning, returning, repentance. Landed at the pre-chorus three-fold. The verb of coming back is the only verb the listener has authority to conjugate for herself.
- Lev Nishbar (לֵב נִשְׁבָּר) — broken heart. Landed at the chorus high note. The Psalm 51:17 sacrifice Yahuah will not despise.
