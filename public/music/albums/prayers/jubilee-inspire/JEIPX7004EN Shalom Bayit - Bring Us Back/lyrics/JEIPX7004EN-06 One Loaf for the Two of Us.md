SONG-LEVEL EMOTIONAL ARC

- Establish: The listener stands before the Table of Showbread and sees a single loaf where she expected two — one for her, one for the silent one
- Escalate: Yeshua sits down between the chairs and breaks the single loaf; the two halves are still one Body; communion is reconciliation; the table for one cannot stay the table for one once He sits down
- Elevate: The Faith Declaration Bridge declares that whenever the listener takes Communion praying for the estranged one, she has already shared bread with them in Mashiach — the table was set for two from the beginning

ANCHOR SCRIPTURES (TWO-WITNESS CONFIRMATION)

- Tanakh: Leviticus 24:5-9 — "thou shalt take fine flour, and bake twelve cakes thereof — and thou shalt set them in two rows, six on a row, upon the pure table before Yahuah continually"; with Psalm 23:5 — "Thou preparest a table before me in the presence of mine enemies"
- Brit Hadashah: 1 Corinthians 10:17 — "for we being many are one bread, and one body — for we are all partakers of that one bread"; with Luke 22:19 — "this is My body which is given for you — this do in remembrance of Me"
- Personalized translation used in lyric: "Yeshua, You said we being many are one bread because we share the one Loaf — and when I take the Bread praying for her, I have already shared the Loaf with her, even when she is not in the room; the table was set for two from the beginning"

---

SENSORY ANCHOR INVENTORY

1. A single warm loaf on a candlelit table set for two
2. The act of breaking bread — the sound, the texture, the smell of recent baking
3. The empty chair across the table that has a place setting laid for it
4. The hand of Yeshua reaching across the table to put bread in both mouths from the same Loaf

---

HEBREW WORD AT PEAK

- Lechem HaPanim (לֶחֶם הַפָּנִים) — bread of the Presence / bread of the Faces. Landed at the chorus. The Bread is the Face of Mashiach turned toward both hungry souls at the same time.
