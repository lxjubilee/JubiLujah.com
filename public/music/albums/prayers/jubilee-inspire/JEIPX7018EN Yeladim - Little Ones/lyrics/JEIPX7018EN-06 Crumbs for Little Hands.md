SONG-LEVEL EMOTIONAL ARC

- Establish: A mother pinches a piece of bread into pieces small enough for a toddler's hand to grasp
- Escalate: The Canaanite mother who asked for crumbs for her daughter and got the Master Himself — the prayer of every parent who has ever asked for less than the whole answer
- Elevate: The revelation that Yeshua broke Himself small enough for the smallest mouth in the house — the broken Bread of His body fits the broken places in a small life

ANCHOR SCRIPTURES (TWO-WITNESS CONFIRMATION)

- Tanakh: Deuteronomy 8:3 — "man does not live by bread alone, but by every word that proceeds out of the mouth of Yahuah does man live"
- Brit Hadashah: Matthew 15:25-28 — the Canaanite mother who said "even the dogs eat of the crumbs which fall from their master's table"; Yeshua answered, "O woman, great is your faith; be it done to you as you desire" and her daughter was healed
- Personalized translation used in lyric: "You said, Yahuah, we live by every word that proceeds from Your mouth — and Yeshua broke the bread small enough for a child, and the Canaanite mother who asked for crumbs for her daughter got the whole Master and the whole healing — and I am asking the same way tonight"

because she knew what every mother knows:

She did not need to be at the table.
She did not need to be from the right tribe.
She needed the master to break the bread small,
and her daughter went home well.

I am asking You the same way she asked,
