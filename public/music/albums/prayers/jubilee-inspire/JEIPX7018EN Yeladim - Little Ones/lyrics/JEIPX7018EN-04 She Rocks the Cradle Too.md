SONG-LEVEL EMOTIONAL ARC

- Establish: A parent in the dark room at 3 AM realizes the rocking chair is not creaking by itself — Someone else is rocking the cradle too
- Escalate: Ruach HaKodesh hovering over the small one the way the eagle hovers over her young, the way She hovered over the deep at creation
- Elevate: The revelation that the parent has never been alone in the room; there has always been Another Mother present — She hovers, She fills, She is the Comforter of small ones first

ANCHOR SCRIPTURES (TWO-WITNESS CONFIRMATION)

- Tanakh: Genesis 1:2 — "the Ruach Elohim was hovering (m'rachefet) over the face of the deep"; Deuteronomy 32:11 — "as an eagle stirs up her nest, hovers over her young, spreads abroad her wings, takes them, bears them on her wings"
- Brit Hadashah: John 14:16-17 — "I will pray the Father, and He will give you another Comforter (Paraclete), that He may abide with you forever; even the Ruach of truth"
- Personalized translation used in lyric: "You said, Yahuah, that Your Ruach hovered over the deep like an eagle hovers over her young — and Yeshua said He would send another Comforter who would abide forever — and tonight the Ruach is hovering over my cradle the way She once hovered over the chaos before the world was anything"

---

SENSORY ANCHOR INVENTORY

1. The faint creak of the rocking chair that the parent did not start rocking
2. The temperature of the room going warmer for no reason at 3:14 AM
3. The shadow of wings stretching across the wall when no bird is in the house
4. The sound of breathing — too many breaths in the room for the number of people in the room

---

HEBREW WORD AT PEAK

- Ruach (רוּחַ) — Spirit, breath, wind. Landed at chorus. The feminine pronoun is load-bearing — She hovers, She fills, She rocks.

HEBREW DICTIONARY

- Amen — "so be it" — a verb of faith-sealing
- Avinu — "our Father"
- Brit Hadashah — "the New Covenant" — the New Testament
- Devarim — Deuteronomy
- Echad — "One" — the unity confessed in the Shema
- Elohim — "God" — the plural-of-majesty Name of the Creator
- Hineni — "here I am" — the surrender word of Avraham, Moshe, and Yeshayahu when Yahuah called
- Mashiach — "Anointed One" / Messiah
- Niggun — wordless melodic prayer-song (lai-lai-lai)
- Ruach — "spirit, breath, wind"
- Ruach HaKodesh — "the Holy Spirit" (Ha- already means "the" — never write "the Ruach HaKodesh")
- Shekhinah — the dwelling, manifest Presence of Yahuah
- Shema — "Hear" — opening word of the great Hebrew confession (Deuteronomy 6:4)
- Tanakh — the Hebrew Scriptures — Torah, Prophets, and Writings
- Tehillim — Psalms
- the Lamb — Yeshua as the sacrificial Lamb of Yahuah (Revelation; cf. John 1:29)
- Yaakov — Jacob, later called Yisrael
- Yahuah — the sacred four-letter Name of the Father (often rendered "the LORD" in English translations)
- Yeladim — "children, little ones"
- Yeshua — the Hebrew name of Jesus, meaning "salvation" or "Yahuah saves"
