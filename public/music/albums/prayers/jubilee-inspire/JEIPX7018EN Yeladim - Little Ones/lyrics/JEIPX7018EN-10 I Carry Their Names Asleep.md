[Intro]

Aharon walked into Your Holy Place, Avinu,
with twelve names over his heart —
twelve stones, twelve gems, twelve tribes,
a memorial before You continually.
And tonight at three AM I am Aharon, Yahuah —
my hallway is the corridor of Your Mishkan,
and my chest is the Choshen,
and the names of the children I am praying for are the stones.

I am walking the hallway like Aharon walked the Tent,
and the names are on my chest tonight.

every hidden name on my breastplate laid open and true.

The one You gave me through the womb — I carry her by name.

- Yeladim — "children, little ones"
- Yeshayahu — Isaiah
- Yeshua — the Hebrew name of Jesus, meaning "salvation" or "Yahuah saves"
- Yisrael — Israel; the people of Yahuah
