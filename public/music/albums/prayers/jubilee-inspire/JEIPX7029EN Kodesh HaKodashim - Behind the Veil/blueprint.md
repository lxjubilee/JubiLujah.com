# JEIPX7029EN — Kodesh HaKodashim - Behind the Veil

Album Code: JEIPX7029EN
Album Title: Kodesh HaKodashim - Behind the Veil
Persona: Jubilee Inspire
Album Type: Prayer Album (Tefillah B'Shir, 12-Station Temple Journey)
Content Mode: OHI (Yahuah / Yeshua / Ruach HaKodesh — feminine pronouns)
Total Runtime: 60:00 (12 × 5:00 — one hour with Yahuah)

Anchor Pairing (Tanakh + Brit Hadashah):
- Leviticus 16:2 — Aaron is warned not to come at all times into the Holy Place inside the veil, lest he die, for Yahuah appears in the cloud over the Mercy Seat.
- Hebrews 10:19-22 — Therefore, brothers, having boldness to enter the Holy Place by the blood of Yeshua, by a new and living way which He inaugurated for us through the veil — that is, His flesh — let us draw near with a true heart in full assurance of faith.

THEME / MOOD DIRECTION

Bold access to the Holy Place through Yeshua. The Parokhet torn from top to bottom (Matthew 27:51) is the central image of the entire album. Once-a-year priest access becoming every-believer-every-moment access. The album reframes reverence: boldness does not diminish reverence — boldness intensifies it. The closer the singer comes, the lower she bows; the more freely she enters, the more trembling she sings. The album ends not at the Mercy Seat but seated on the floor inside the Holy of Holies — which is now her seat too.

================================================================
THREE-ACT MAPPING
================================================================

ACT 1 — Approach (Tracks 01-03 / Stations 1-3)
The singer comes to the threshold knowing that behind the veil is what she was made for. Acts of gratitude, repentance, and washing precede every step inward. The torn-veil reality is named in Act 1 but not yet entered.

ACT 2 — Crossing (Tracks 04-09 / Stations 4-9)
The singer crosses the outer veil into the Holy Place, learns intimacy with Ruach HaKodesh, is illuminated at the Menorah, fed at the Shewbread, anointed at the Incense Altar, then INVITED through the second veil at S8 (cinematic revelation). At S9 she bows before the Mercy Seat that was once forbidden.

ACT 3 — Living from Inside (Tracks 10-12 / Stations 10-12)
The singer now intercedes for others from inside the Holy of Holies (S10), petitions for herself from inside (S11), and walks back out into the world at S12 still carrying the inside — every-believer-every-moment access made permanent. Sealed Amen lands here.

SHEMA SPINE NOTES
================================================================

- Tracks 01 and 12 sing the Shema in FULL in the Niggun Bridge: "Shema, Yisrael, Yahuah Eloheinu, Yahuah Echad." Track 01 sets it as the threshold confession of Echad ("the One whose courts I am entering"). Track 12 closes it as the sealed confession of Echad ("the One I now live inside"). Track 12 also carries the sole Sealed Amen of the album.
- Tracks 02 through 11 carry the Shema as a hummed melodic fragment within "lai-lai-lai" — recognizable but not fully verbalized.
