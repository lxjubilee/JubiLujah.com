SONG-LEVEL EMOTIONAL ARC

- Establish: The watchman has entered the gates carrying names — and stops at the Bronze Altar realizing he cannot intercede for a gap in another while there is a gap in himself
- Escalate: The fire on the altar burns away the secret thing the watchman did not bring up the wall — the bitterness toward the prodigal, the pride in the standing, the unconfessed root that disqualifies the lifted hand
- Elevate: The blood of Yeshua on this same altar makes the cleansing instant; the watchman rises off the altar lighter, with hands now fit to lift others

ANCHOR SCRIPTURES (TWO-WITNESS CONFIRMATION)

- Tanakh: Psalm 51:10–13 — "Create in me a clean heart, O Elohim; and renew a right ruach within me... then will I teach transgressors Your ways; and sinners shall be converted unto You"
- Brit Hadashah: Romans 12:1 — "I beseech you therefore, brethren, by the mercies of Elohim, that you present your bodies a living sacrifice, holy, acceptable unto Elohim, which is your reasonable service"
- Personalized translation used in lyric: "Create in me the clean heart I need to stand in the gap for them, Yahuah — I cannot teach a transgressor Your ways if a transgression is still teaching me — present my body on this altar as a living sacrifice so the standing is Yours, not mine"

---

SENSORY ANCHOR INVENTORY

1. The heat of the bronze altar fire at chest level when the watchman steps in close
2. The taste of confession in the throat — metallic, like the salt of tears
3. The sound of one thing the watchman did not want to say falling into the fire and crackling
4. The relief of empty hands that can now be lifted for another

HEBREW WORD AT PEAK

- Teshuvah (תְּשׁוּבָה) — turning, returning, repentance. Landed on the Pre-Chorus. The act of turning IS the prayer at that moment.

HEBREW DICTIONARY

- Amen — "so be it" — a verb of faith-sealing
- Avinu — "our Father"

- Brit Hadashah — "the New Covenant" — the New Testament
- Echad — "One" — the unity confessed in the Shema
- Elohim — "God" — the plural-of-majesty Name of the Creator

- Niggun — wordless melodic prayer-song (lai-lai-lai)
- Ruach — "spirit, breath, wind"
- Ruach HaKodesh — "the Holy Spirit" (Ha- already means "the" — never write "the Ruach HaKodesh")
- Shekhinah — the dwelling, manifest Presence of Yahuah
- Shema — "Hear" — opening word of the great Hebrew confession (Deuteronomy 6:4)
- Tanakh — the Hebrew Scriptures — Torah, Prophets, and Writings
- Teshuvah — "turning, returning" — repentance
- the Lamb — Yeshua as the sacrificial Lamb of Yahuah (Revelation; cf. John 1:29)
- Yahuah — the sacred four-letter Name of the Father (often rendered "the LORD" in English translations)
- Yeshua — the Hebrew name of Jesus, meaning "salvation" or "Yahuah saves"
- Yochanan — John
