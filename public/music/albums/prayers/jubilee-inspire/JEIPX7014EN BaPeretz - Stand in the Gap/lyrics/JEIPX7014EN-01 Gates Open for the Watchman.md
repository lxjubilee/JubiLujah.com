SONG-LEVEL EMOTIONAL ARC

- Establish: A watchman comes off the wall to enter the Temple — carrying a list of names that are not their own — expecting the gates to bar him because he is bringing the unworthy
- Escalate: The gates swing WIDER for the watchman than for the solo worshipper — because the courts of Yahuah were built for the priest who brings someone else in
- Elevate: The Shema is sung in full by the communal ensemble — many watchmen confess Echad together while crossing the threshold; the names have not been answered for YET but the watchmen and their names have already been received

- Personalized translation used in lyric: "I am entering Your gates with the names I have been watching for, Yahuah — I am crossing into Your courts with the souls I have been giving account for — and the gate swings wider because I am not coming alone"

[Intro]
(whispered, sparse cello drone, single ney flute breath, distant footsteps on stone)
Yahuah... Avinu... I am at the gate...

[Verse 1]
I come down from the wall, Avinu,
where I have been watching since the second watch.
I have a list folded against my chest —
names I cannot stop carrying,
a husband, a child, a friend on a hospital floor,
a city that does not know it is on fire.
And I expect Your gate to be bolted
because the names I bring are not yet washed.
But Your lamps are still burning
and Your gate swings wider for the watchman
than for the worshipper who comes alone.

because the Blood of the Lamb on the doorpost

HEBREW DICTIONARY

- Aaronic Blessing — the priestly blessing of Numbers 6:24-26 (You bless me and keep me, You make Your face to shine upon me...)
- Amen — "so be it" — a verb of faith-sealing
- Avinu — "our Father"
- BaPeretz — "in the breach" (Ezekiel 22:30 — standing in the gap)
- Brit Hadashah — "the New Covenant" — the New Testament
- Echad — "One" — the unity confessed in the Shema
- Hineni — "here I am" — the surrender word of Avraham, Moshe, and Yeshayahu when Yahuah called
- Niggun — wordless melodic prayer-song (lai-lai-lai)
- Ruach HaKodesh — "the Holy Spirit" (Ha- already means "the" — never write "the Ruach HaKodesh")
- Shekhinah — the dwelling, manifest Presence of Yahuah
- Shema — "Hear" — opening word of the great Hebrew confession (Deuteronomy 6:4)
- Tanakh — the Hebrew Scriptures — Torah, Prophets, and Writings
- the Lamb — Yeshua as the sacrificial Lamb of Yahuah (Revelation; cf. John 1:29)
- Yahuah — the sacred four-letter Name of the Father (often rendered "the LORD" in English translations)
- Yahuah Echad — "Yahuah is One" (the Shema affirmation)
- Yahuah Eloheinu — "Yahuah our God" (from the Shema)
- Yeshayahu — Isaiah
- Yeshua — the Hebrew name of Jesus, meaning "salvation" or "Yahuah saves"
- Yisrael — Israel; the people of Yahuah
