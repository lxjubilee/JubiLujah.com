the same gate that swung wide for the watchman

The wall has held, Yahuah.
The breach is sealed in the heavenlies
even if it still looks like a breach in the natural —
because You have heard,
and where You have heard,
there is an answer already moving through the city.

The wall has held —
shalom, shalom, Your shalom, Yahuah.

[Verse 3]

every hidden room laid open and true.
