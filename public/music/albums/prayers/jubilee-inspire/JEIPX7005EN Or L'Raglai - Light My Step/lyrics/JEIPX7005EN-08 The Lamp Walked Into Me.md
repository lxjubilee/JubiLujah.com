SONG-LEVEL EMOTIONAL ARC

- Establish: A believer stands at the inner veil - the Parokhet - and recalls she has been asking Yahuah to hand her a lamp from outside
- Escalate: The cinematic revelation - the Or HaOlam, the Light of the world, did not stay overhead like a chandelier; Yeshua walked Himself into her chest the day His head bowed; she IS the lantern, the Lamp is inside her
- Elevate: Guidance flows because the Light is inside the carrier - the next step is illuminated from inside the foot, not from above the road

ANCHOR SCRIPTURES (Two-Witness Confirmation)

- Tanakh: Isaiah 60:19-20 - "The sun shall be no more your light by day; neither for brightness shall the moon give light unto you: but Yahuah shall be unto you an everlasting Light, and your Elohim your glory. Your sun shall no more go down, neither shall your moon withdraw itself: for Yahuah shall be your everlasting Light."
- Brit Hadashah: John 8:12 + Hebrews 10:19-20 - "I am the Light of the world; he that follows Me shall not walk in darkness, but shall have the Light of life... we have boldness to enter into the Most Holy by the blood of Yeshua, by a new and living way which He has consecrated for us, through the veil, that is to say, His flesh."
- Personalized translation used in lyric: "Yeshua said He is the Light of the world - and the day the veil tore, the Light did not stay overhead like a chandelier - the Light walked Himself into my chest. I am the lantern. The Or HaOlam is inside me. Guidance lights the foot from the inside out."

SENSORY ANCHOR INVENTORY

1. The sound of fabric tearing - slow, from the top, heavy
2. The visual of a thick curtain split in two, gold light pouring through
3. The sensation of warm Light walking through the chest the way breath walks through lungs
4. The weight of standing where only the High Priest stood, and finding the room is the room inside your own chest

HEBREW DICTIONARY

- Aharon — Aaron — the first High Priest, who carried twelve names on his breastplate
- Amen — "so be it" — a verb of faith-sealing
- Avinu — "our Father"
- Brit Hadashah — "the New Covenant" — the New Testament
- Echad — "One" — the unity confessed in the Shema
- Elohim — "God" — the plural-of-majesty Name of the Creator
- HaMashiach — "the Messiah" (Ha- is Hebrew "the")
- Hineni — "here I am" — the surrender word of Avraham, Moshe, and Yeshayahu when Yahuah called
- Mashiach — "Anointed One" / Messiah
- Niggun — wordless melodic prayer-song (lai-lai-lai)
- Or — "light"
- Or L'Raglai — "a lamp for my feet" (Psalm 119:105)
- Parokhet — the inner veil of the Temple, torn at Calvary (Matthew 27:51)
- Ruach HaKodesh — "the Holy Spirit" (Ha- already means "the" — never write "the Ruach HaKodesh")
- Shekhinah — the dwelling, manifest Presence of Yahuah
- Shema — "Hear" — opening word of the great Hebrew confession (Deuteronomy 6:4)
- Tanakh — the Hebrew Scriptures — Torah, Prophets, and Writings
- the Lamb — Yeshua as the sacrificial Lamb of Yahuah (Revelation; cf. John 1:29)
- Yahuah — the sacred four-letter Name of the Father (often rendered "the LORD" in English translations)
- Yeshayahu — Isaiah
- Yeshua — the Hebrew name of Jesus, meaning "salvation" or "Yahuah saves"
