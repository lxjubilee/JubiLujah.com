SONG-LEVEL EMOTIONAL ARC

- Establish: A believer kneels at the bronze altar with twelve folded paper maps and a handful of broken plastic compasses she drew and used to guide herself for years
- Escalate: The admission that every map she drew without Him became an idol; the burning starts, slow and consuming, and the bronze fire does not consume the body but the cartography wrapped around it
- Elevate: The Faith Declaration Bridge crosses from confession to receipt - the maps are ash; the believer is undone; the only direction left in her hand is the one Yahuah will give

- Tanakh: Proverbs 3:5-6 - "Trust in Yahuah with all your heart, and lean not on your own understanding. In all your ways acknowledge Him, and He shall direct your paths."
- Brit Hadashah: Romans 12:1-2 - "I beseech you therefore, brethren, by the mercies of Elohim, that you present your bodies a living sacrifice, holy, acceptable unto Elohim, which is your reasonable service. And be not conformed to this world, but be transformed by the renewing of your mind, that you may prove what is that good and acceptable and perfect will of Elohim."

Tonight I lay every map I drew without You on the altar - and the bronze fire knows the difference between me and the maps."

LYRICS

[Intro]
(whispered, sparse cello drone, faint crackle of paper catching fire)
Avinu, El Elyon, Yahuah Tzva'ot...
I come to Your bronze altar...
With every map I drew without You...
I lay them down...

[Verse 1]
I drew twelve maps, Yahuah,
in twelve seasons of pretending I could see.
I sanctified my shortcuts.
I named my own way after Your will.
I leaned on my own understanding
the way Mishlei warned me not to,
and I called the leaning faith.
I bought a counterfeit compass at every fork in the road -
and tonight I am laying them on the bronze.

[Pre-Chorus]
Teshuvah, Yahuah - I am turning back.
Your bronze altar fire is older than my maps.
By the blood of the Lamb who already burned in my place,
the wood is dry, the fire is ready -
burn the counterfeit compass, Avinu, burn it down.

[Chorus]
Burn the counterfeit compass, Avinu -
the cartography I drew when I would not ask You -
I lay it down, let it burn,
I lay it down, it is burning.
Your fire will not eat the body,
Your fire has eaten the maps -
light my step, just my step,
in Your bronze fire, Avinu.

[Verse 2]
I drew the road to my own house
and called it the road You wanted me on.
I drew the door I wanted opened
and pretended the door knew Your Name.
I drew a marriage, drew a calling,
drew a budget, drew a yes -
and I was so afraid of the dark
I forgot the dark is where You speak.
Forgive me, Yahuah - the maps are on the altar.

[Pre-Chorus]
Teshuvah, Yahuah - I am turning back.
Your bronze altar fire is older than my maps.
By the blood of the Lamb who already burned in my place,
the wood is dry, the fire is ready -
burn the counterfeit compass, Avinu, burn it down.

[Chorus]
Burn the counterfeit compass, Avinu -
the cartography I drew when I would not ask You -
I lay it down, let it burn,
I lay it down, it is burning.
Your fire will not eat the body,
Your fire has eaten the maps -
light my step, just my step,
in Your bronze fire, Avinu.

[Instrumental]
(violin niggun, mournful and ornamented, ney flute response, oud counterpoint - 30 seconds; the faint crackle of fire continues underneath)

[Verse 3]
Hineni, Avinu - here I am, undone before You,
every hidden room laid open and true.
The maps are ash now.
The compasses are slag now.
My hand is empty and my heart is empty -
and an empty hand is the only hand
that can hold the lamp You are about to light.
Receive me without my drawings.
Receive me without my plans.
Receive me with nothing but the asking.

[Bridge]
(Faith Declaration Bridge - finished-reality, present tense, rising)
By Your Word, it is done.
By Your stripes, I am re-pathed.
By the blood of the Lamb, the maps are ash and I have access.
In Yeshua's Name, I have received Your direction in place of my own.

(Niggun Bridge - wordless lai-lai-lai with Shema melodic fragment hummed)
Lai-lai-lai... lai-lai-lai...
(Shema melody contour hummed)
Teshuvah... Echad... lai-lai-lai...

[Refrain]
(Proverbs 3:5-6 personalized)
I trust You, Yahuah, with all my heart,
I lean not on my own understanding,
in all my ways I acknowledge You -
and You direct my paths.
I trust You, Yahuah, with all my heart.

[Final Chorus]
(full ensemble, weeping strings, layered Shekhinah harmonies, the fire crackling underneath)
Burn the counterfeit compass, Avinu -
the cartography I drew when I would not ask You -
I lay it down, let it burn,
I lay it down, it is burning.
Your fire will not eat the body,
Your fire has eaten the maps -
light my step, just my step,
in Your bronze fire, Avinu.

[Outro]

(breath returning to silence, the last crackle of paper)

(Whisper-of-Elijah - almost inaudible)
"There is a way which seems right unto a man, but the end thereof are the ways of death." - Mishlei 14:12
