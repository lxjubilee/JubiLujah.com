Role-Based Access Control
Roles & permissions
A minimum role of content_editor is required to access Jubilujah.
Role Permissions
viewer Read-only catalog access (no edits, no ratings, no comments).
content_editorMinimum Full CRUD on songs, albums, lyrics, metadata; rate; comment; nominate.
radio_producer All content_editor permissions + manage programs, playlists, and station assignments.
production_manager All radio_producer permissions + advance songs through pipeline stages; approve stage gates.
admin All production_manager permissions + manage users/roles; select award winners; trigger publishes.
