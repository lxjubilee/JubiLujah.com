# Updated Album Titles — Before → After

Albums whose **title was changed** after the original artwork/folder was created — chiefly the cultural-compliance renames (Amir: interfaith / Sufi themes → Christian; Zev: Judaica → Messianic; Tahoma: Native-ceremonial → worship).

- **Before** = the album's original title, recovered from its folder slug and verified against the title printed on the delivered cover art where available.
- **After** = the current catalog title (`app/web/public/music/catalog-manifest.json`).
- Diacritic-only, punctuation-only, ampersand, added-gloss, and stopword-only differences are **excluded** (not real renames).
