## BLUEPRINT ARCHITECTURE STANDARD

Every album follows consistent 8-section mandatory structure:

1. **Enhanced Theme Statement** (800-1200 words)
   - Spiritual/emotional context for album
   - Theological positioning
   - Sonic integration plan
   - Listener transformation promise

2. **Genre Fusion Laboratory** (detailed sonic DNA extraction)
   - Parent genres identified (3-4 minimum)
   - Fusion methodology explained
   - Sonic characteristics per genre

3. **Album Vision** (table format)
   - Title, thesis, content domain, target audience
   - Transformation promise, viability rating
   - Award positioning, hit single density

4. **Listener Wounds & Promises** (healing/transformation narrative)
   - What listeners enter carrying
   - What transformation they'll experience
   - Integration promise by album end

5. **Twelve Subthemes** (narrative arc with ratings 5.5-10/10)
   - Title, theme description, intensity rating
   - Establishes 12-track structural arc
   - Identifies hit singles and climax moments

6. **Track-by-Track Blueprint** (granular specifications)
   - Vocal range, key, BPM, instrumentation
   - Production philosophy, emotional arc
   - Each track's theological/emotional positioning

7. **Album Arc Summary** (listener journey map)
   - Emotional progression across 12 tracks
   - Transformation moments identified
   - Integration points marked

8. **Production & Deployment Notes** (technical/strategic)
   - Viability and transformation ratings
   - Next phase specifications
   - Quality standards confirmation
